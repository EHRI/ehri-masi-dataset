Oral history interview with Adriaan van As

5 videocassettes (DVCAM), sound, color ; 1/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. The interview was directed and supervised by Nathan Beyrak. The interview was conducted with Adriaan van As in the Netherlands for the Netherlands Documentation Project on July 19, 2004. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in May 2005.
