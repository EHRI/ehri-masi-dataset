Collection of newsletters, newspaper clippings, black and white negatives and photographs, documents, glass slides, magazine, postcards, calling card and 16 mm film reels. Col. Alexander Zabin was a US Army surgeon and carried his own camera with him into the field, filming his company and mash unit, performing field surgery, and liberating death camps in the European theater of war. Also includes four hours of Nazi scientific/cultural/educational films.
