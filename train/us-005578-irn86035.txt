Oral history interview with Klaus Schoppe and Anita Schoppe

4 , MPEG-2

Shloime Perel, on behalf of the Refugee Research Project associated with McGill University, donated copies of Gabrielle Tyrnauer's oral history interview with Klaus Schoppe and Anita Schoppe to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2013. Gabrielle Tyrnauer produced the interview as part of her research on the fate of Romani people during the Holocaust.
