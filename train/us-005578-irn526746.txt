Oral history interview with Renée Sachs

3 digital files, MOV

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Keith Sachs, in loving memory of his wife, donated the oral history interview with Renée Sachs to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in December 2015. The interview was produced in 2007 and 2008 in New Jersey by North Brunswick Township high school students Hillary Brady, Elizabeth Chelli, and Elizabeth Tsygane under the guidance of teacher Beth Feinberg Passner, in association with the Second Generation Holocaust Education Fund's Adopt-A-Survivor program.
