Registro 139 F (1937-38)

Approximately 200 pages

School register of female students of public Mantua schools, including also “Rosa Maltoni Mussolini” elementary school, located in Chiassi street. Among the students of the teacher Laura Lentini Sgarbi, there was Luisa Levi, born in Mantua on November, 10th, 1929, from Jewish parents (Samuele and Elide Levi). She had very high marks. 
She will be the youngest among the Mantuan Jews deported to the concentration camps (on April, 5th, 1944); she will later die in Bergen-Belsen.
