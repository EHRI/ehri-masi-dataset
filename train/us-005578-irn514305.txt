Kurt and Helen Rosendahl collection

folder

1

Collection consists of four photographs, one 2 RM note from the Buchenwald SS commisary, three documents declaring K. Rosendahl a former political prisoner (from the Amicale of Buchenwald, the National Federation of World War II political prisoners, and from the Brussels Minister of the Reconstruction), two photocopies of identifying documentation, one article by Kurt Rosendahl entitled "Buchenwald Revisited (2 pages), and one exhibition brochure for "The Overlooked Holocaust: The Devastation of the Sephardic Communities," which ran from 11/16/92-6/24/93 at the Holocaust Resource Center and Archives at the Queensborough Community College, CUNY.
