Oral history interview with Alfred Lipson

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Harriet and Kenneth Kupferberg Holocaust Center at Queensborough Community College, CUNY

The interview with Alfred Lipson was conducted on April 29, 1985 by the Queensborough Community College Holocaust Resource Center and Archives. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received a copy of the interview in November 1990 and an interviewee release form on April 2, 1991.
