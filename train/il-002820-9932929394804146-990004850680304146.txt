[Bern trial - duplication of judicial transcripts concerning mostly Fleischhauer]

1 electronic resource (68 pages)

This file contains 68 pages including several duplicated documents that have been used for the Bern trials. All of the original documents have been written in the time between 1935 and 1936. They contain letters from Fleischhauer to the judge at the Bern trial as well as several judicial transcripts against Fleischhauer and from Fleischhauer himself against the court, suing them.
