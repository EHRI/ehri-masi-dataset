Oral history interview with Nelly Toll

2 videocassettes (Digital Betacam), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Documentary filmmaker, Aviva Slesin, conducted the oral history interview with Nelly Toll on September 15, 2000 for her film, Secret Lives: Hidden Children and Their Rescuers During WWII. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the interview in June 2006.
