Oral history interview with Simon Taitz

2 sound cassettes (60 min.),

American Gathering of Jewish Holocaust Survivors conducted the interview with Simon Taitz on April 12, 1983, in Washington, D.C., during the American Gathering of Jewish Holocaust Survivors Conference. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Dept. received the tapes of the interview in 1989. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes by transfer in February 1995.
