Later beschrijft hij hoe de Amsterdamse politie samenwerkte met de Duitse bezetter, geeft commentaar op de rol van de politie bij het oppakken en deporteren van Joden en legt hij uit wat zijn rol was als lid van het Nederlandse verzet. In this interview the interviewee, a former policeman, talks about his work in Amsterdam at the time of the German occupation. He focuses on how, from being a small town boy, he went from the army into the police force. In a later stage he describes how the Amsterdam police force collaborated with the German occupier, comments on the role of the police force in the round-up and deportation of Jews and explains his role as a member of the Dutch resistance.
