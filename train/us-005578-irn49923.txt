Oral history interview with Rašela Knežević Levi

1 digital files, MPEG-2

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. Wendy Lower, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, coordinated the interview with Rašela Knežević Levi in Germany on July 19, 2009 and July 20, 2009. The interview was received by the Museum's Oral History Branch in 2011.
