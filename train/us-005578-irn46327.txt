United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Collection of loose photo album pages and loose photographs documenting the donor and his family's experiences in Shanghai, China and Kobe, Japan during WWII, Includes an identification card issued to Kurt Marcus from the Deutsche Lebens-Rettungs-Gesellschaft E.V. certifying him in life rescue, dated August 23, 1933, in German; and a 1995 memoir documenting the experiences of Kurt Marcus, in English
