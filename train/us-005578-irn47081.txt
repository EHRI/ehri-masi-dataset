Selected records of the National Land Mortgage Bank of Hungary (MOL Z)

83,605 digital images, JPEG

24 microfilm reels (digitized), 16 mm

Magyar Országos Levéltár

Documents from various offices of the National Land Mortgage Bank of Hungary (Országos Földhitelintézet) : Economic/Business (reports on fisheries, land leases); Estate Policy, 1911‒1947 (maps, legal documents); Executive Council, 1899‒1947 (minutes, correspondence); Personnel; Executive (correspondence); Legal (sentencing records, correspondence, records of loans, legal fees, court requests, affidavits, etcetera). Documents of the National Central Credit Union (Országos Központi Hitelszövetkezet), including the department of Internal Investigation (personnel files, correspondence about finance, loan records), and the Office of Retirement (correspondence regarding rights of heirs). Documents of the National Housing Credit Cooperative (Országos Lakásépítési Hitelszövetkezet ), including ID cards related to finance files and correspondence.
