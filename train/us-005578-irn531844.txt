Archiwum Państwowe w Kielcach

The Bank Spółdzielczy Właścicieli Nieruchomości w Kielcach was established in Kielce on October 8, 1928. Its objective was to enable taking credit and investing funds in own credible institution. The Board was chaired by Stanisław Rotman while the Supervisory Board was headed by Moszek Ajzenber.

Selected records of one of the Jewish banks in Kielce, including are organizational files, minutes of sessions, lists of members, registers, books of assets, shares and financial files.
