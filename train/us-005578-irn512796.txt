Jüdisches Regionalmuseum Mittelfranken in Fürth collection

2 microfilm reels, 35 mm

Jüdisches Museum Franken

Contains personal papers of the Gräfenberg family, Schäler family, and Lotte Roman (n.d.); correspondence of Lore Sulzbacher with her parents who were deported to Theresienstadt (1938-1944); and documents pertaining to the Spear toy factory (1938-1940).
