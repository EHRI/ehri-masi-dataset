Oral history interview with Mirjam Pinkhof

2 sound cassettes (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Mirjam Pinkhof in Haifa, Israel, on July 8, 1989. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on September 30, 1999.
