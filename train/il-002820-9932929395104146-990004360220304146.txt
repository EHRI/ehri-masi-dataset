[Photos of resettlement of Yugoslavian children for Lebensborn]

1 electronic resource (3 Pages)

The file contains the documentation of the resettlement if Yugoslavian children in 1942. Three photos show how the children / infants in baskets, leave the train and enter the station of Frohnleiten in Austria, accompanied by nurses and German field police. The children have been taken away from their parents to go to Lebensborn homes. The parents, allegedly partisans, have been killed or sent to a concentration camp.
