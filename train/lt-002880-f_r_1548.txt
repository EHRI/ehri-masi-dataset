Vilniaus apygardos komisarinis tarėjas

One subfonds and 4 files. Some of the files are microfilmed.

Documents and orders of the German authorities and Lithuanian administration, reports of the chiefs of the districts about situation in the districts, orders and correspondence about nationalized Jewish property, registration of Jewish property; lists of property and equipment of Jewish doctors, registration and information about synagogues in the district and about cultural heritage, lists of Jewish doctors, etc.; register lists of the Auxiliary police, payment lists for local residents for their help with confiscated Jewish property (for example: for sorting of the property, cleaning, transporting to the storehouse, etc.), the report of the chief of Švenčionys County to the Commissar councillor of the Vilnius region concerning establishing of the ghetto and situation in the ghetto on December 1941.
