Oral history interview with Adele Silber

2 videocassettes (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Adele Silber on October 14, 1992 and August 20, 1993. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in October 2000.
