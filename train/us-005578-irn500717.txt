Rosa Shoshana Taubamn Budick (later Shoshana Budick) was born in Kraków, Poland, on 29 December 1921. She immigrated to Israel after World War II where she married and had two children.

Testimony and one photograph describing Rosa Shoshana Taubamn Budick’s (later Shoshana Budick) family and childhood in Kraków as well as life in the Kraków ghetto.
