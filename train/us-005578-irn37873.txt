Flint family papers

folder

1

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Collection of family photographs that include images of the Flint family in Piaseczno, Poland; Malka Perla Flint's (donor's mother) arrival to Columbia; Lezer Muchawsky and Malka Perla Flint's (donor's parents) wedding photograph; a family portrait taken in Bogota; and a postcard sent from Poland to Bogata, postmarked March 27, 1941, in Yiddish.
