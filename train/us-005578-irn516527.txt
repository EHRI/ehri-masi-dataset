Agata Bubola papers

folder

The papers consist of a birth certificate ("Geburtskunde") issued for Gabor Müller in Feldsberg, Austria (now Valtice, Czech Republic) (Gabor Müller died six weeks after his birth) and a postcard written by Agata Müller [donor] in Feldsberg, Austria, to Dr. Josef Rado [donor's father] in Mährisch-Kromau (Moravsky Krumlov), Czech Republic.
