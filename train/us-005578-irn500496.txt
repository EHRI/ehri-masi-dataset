Sender Lipfeld papers

folder

1

Sender Lipfeld was a survivor of the Holocaust who suffered in several Russian prisons. He lost several relatives in both Auschwitz and Treblinka.

Assortment of material, including news clippings about Josef Mengele from 1980s, booklet from Sachsenhausen memorial site in 1960s-1970s, some items related to Siegfried Halbreich (cover of memoirs, invite sent to him), anti-P.L.O. flyer from 1970s.
