"The Battle of the Warsaw Ghetto"

Interviews of survivors of the battle of the Warsaw ghetto

1 CD, WAV

Marlene Sanders donated oral history interviews of survivors of the battle of the Warsaw ghetto to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum on November 5, 2013. The interviews were originally recorded for the WNEW radio broadcast of "The Battle of the Warsaw Ghetto" which aired April 14 and 21, 1963 on Sunday Evening Close-Up. The Wisconsin Historical Society owns the original tape-recordings as part of the collection titled Marlene Sanders Papers, 1933-2006, while CBS News Information Resources maintains copyright to the recording.
