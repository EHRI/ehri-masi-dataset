Mania Baghdadi was born in 1946 in Pieszyce, Poland, to Josef and Bela Kleinburd. Her Father served with the Polish and Soviet armies during World War II, and her mother survived the Holocaust in Opatow, Skarzysko Kamienna, Czestochowa, Liepaja, Leipzig, and Buchenwald. They lived in the displaced persons camps at Regensburg, Lager Lechfeld, Giebelstadt, and Vilseck after the war while preparing for immigration.

Consists of a photograph of Mania Baghdadi (at approximately four years old) holding a doll given to her at a displaced persons camp in Germany.
