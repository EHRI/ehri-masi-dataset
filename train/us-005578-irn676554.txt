Jewish County Committee in Żary

Powiatowy Komitet Żydowski w Żarach (Sygn. 366)

6,411 digital images, PDF

Żydowski Instytut Historyczny im. Emanuela Ringelbluma

Żary is a medium-sized city in southwestern Poland. Jews settled there after the Second World War, in 1946, They were mainly repatriates who survived the German occupation in the Soviet Union. The settlers created a Jewish Committee which dealt with the search for relatives, social assistance and other current affairs of the Jewish community.

Ordinances, announcements, minutes of meetings, official correspondence, registration questionnaires of members, personal files of committee employees, materials related to the Youth Department and others.
