Yiddish Intermediate School in Plungé

Plungės žydų vidurinė mokykla (Fond 781)

5,349 digital images, JPEG

Lietuvos centrinis valstybės archyvas

Jewish Secondary School was established in Plungė, Lithuania on August 15, 1926. The primary language of the instruction was Hebrew.

Minutes of teachers' council meetings, examinations records; copies of graduation certificates, including photos of students; class journals with the names of students and their grades.
