[Translation of the verdict of the Radio Islam Trial in Sweden]

40 pages

The file contains a translation (swedish to english) of the verdict of the Radio Islam Trial of 1989 in Stockholm. Subject of the case is the so-called Radio Islam which was founded by Ahmed Rami and broadcasted antisemitic and antizionistic contents in the Swedish Community Radio network. In November 1989 the court found Ahmed Rami as guilty and convicted him to six months imprisonment.
