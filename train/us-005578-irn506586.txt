Oral history interview with Waclaw Kolodziejek

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Bozenna M. Urbanowicz-Gilbride conducted the interview with Woclaw Kolodziejek on August 5, 1994 in Woodside, NY. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tape of the interview from Mrs. Urbanowicz-Gilbride on July 1, 1996.
