Oral history interview with Isaac Breiter

1 CD-ROM,

Asociacion Mutual Israelita Argentina-Comunidad de Buenos Aires - Centro de Documentatión e Information sobre Judaismo Argentino "Marc Turkow"

The Centro de Documentatión e Information sobre Judaismo Argentino "Marc Turkow" of the Asociacion Mutual Israelita Argentina-Comunidad de Buenos Aires (AMIA) donated a copy of its oral history interview with Isaac Breiter to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in August 2008.
