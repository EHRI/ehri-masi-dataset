"Jewish Secular Culture and Political Commitment"

Oral testimony of Izio Rosenman

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Isabelle Pleskoff, head librarian at the Musée d'art et d'histoire du judaïsme (Mahj), donated video recordings of Holocaust presentations and interviews produced by the Mahj to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 2015. The interview was conducted on May 4, 2014 and the original French title is "Culture juive laïque et engagement politique avec Izio Rosenman."
