Rita Philip papers

1 folder

<div>Fanny Moses, now known as Rita Philip, was a German Jewish émigré living in Amsterdam when the Germans invaded the Netherlands in 1940. Her brother was living in Enschede near the German border. Her mother was still in Germany. Her father was in England where he later served a period of internment. Another brother and sister were already in Palestine.</div><div><br /></div>

This collection comprises an original, undated, though apparently contemporary account of Fanny Moses' journey to the UK from Amsterdam without any money. Also included are a copy of the document, both a manuscript and typescript translation of the document and 2 copy photographs of Fanny Moses
