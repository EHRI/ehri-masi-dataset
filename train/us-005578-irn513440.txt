Oral history interview with Myriam Carmi

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Patricia Benezra/Claudius Communications

The interview with Myriam Carmi was conducted on December 1, 1994 in Tel Aviv, Israel, by Patricia Benezra of Claudius Communications. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received a copy of the interview in December 1995
