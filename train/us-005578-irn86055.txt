Oral history interview with Reinhold Rosenbach

3 , MPEG-2

Fortunoff Video Archive for Holocaust Testimonies

Shloime Perel, on behalf of the Refugee Research Project associated with McGill University, donated copies of Gabrielle Tyrnauer's oral history interview with Reinhold Rosenbach to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2013. Gabrielle Tyrnauer produced the interview on behalf of the Fortunoff Video Archive for Holocaust Testimonies at Yale University.
