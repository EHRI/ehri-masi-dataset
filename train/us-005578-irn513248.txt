Oral history interview with Vern Smith

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Survivors and Surviving Generations of the Holocaust

The interview with Vern Smith was conducted on July 15, 1995, by the Surviving Generations of the Holocaust as part of a project documenting the testimonies of members of the 42nd Rainbow Division whom participated in the liberation of Dachau. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received a copy of the interview in March 1997.
