Consists of a photo album depicting the pre-war work of the Relief Committee of Antwerp and pre-war and wartime photographs of Stalag 10c, from the collection of Leopold Guttman, who was arrested and imprisoned as a member of the Belgian army. Also includes one copy of the Tactical Air Force "Judaean Magazine" of June 1945, which includes an article about Guttman's work to restore the Wunstorf synagogue and a report entitled, "A Brief Picture History of how Leopold Guttman Came to America."
