Photographic postcard: black and white image of four men wearing matching caps, star of David badges and cuff band reading “Ordnungsdienst” visible on three of the four; dated verso June 15, 1941; Łódź ghetto, Poland; Chaim “Hy” Feierman (donors’ father) stands second from right Photographic print: black and white image of large group of men and women seated and standing together, American soldier seated in front center; presumed to be in the Landsberg DP camp; Chaim Feierman stands to the right of the woman standing behind the soldier with a “3" visible over his shoulder; not dated.
