Oral history interview with Freda Silberberg Wineman

11 sound cassettes (60 min.),

The oral history interview forms part of The Living Memory of the Jewish Community collection of the British Library National Sound Archive, 29 Exhibition Rd., London SW7 2 AS, England. Freda Silberberg Wineman sent a copy of the audio cassettes and transcript to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives in December 1993, and signed a donor agreement in November 1995. The British Library National Sound Archive signed a donor agreement for the transcript in November 1995.
