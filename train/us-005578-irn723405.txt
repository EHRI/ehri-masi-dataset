Asociaţia Culturală Minerva

Hungarian language cultural journal began publication on Jan. 1, 1895 in Brassó, Hungary (now Romania), still in publication. The selected issues of the Brassói Lapok journal relate to the period before WWII and focus on the Transylvanian Hungarian attitude toward the anti-Jewish measures adopted by the Romanian Government during the 1930s.
