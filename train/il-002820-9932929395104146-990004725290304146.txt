Propaganda

1 electronic resource (39 pages)

This brochure, published by the propaganda department of the National Socialist German Worker 's Party, is an introduction into the tasks of the propaganda department and of propaganda itself and also gives some important guidelines regarding propaganda. There is no year of publication given, but on the front page there is a handwritten note saying "after 1926". The main topics listed in the table of contents are: Guidelines of propaganda, the board of propaganda, kinds of propaganda, speech, gathering and discussion evening, the press, leaflets, other means of propaganda, fundraising for propaganda, notifications and material for propaganda
