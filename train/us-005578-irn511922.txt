Oral history interview with Galina Iosefovna Klotsman

2 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak, project director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Galina Iosefovna Klotsman in Ukraine on August 6, 1994. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in March 1995.
