Levivka village administration in Pidvysotsk district

8 files

Collection contains orders, instructions from Kirovohrad Gebietskommissariat on administrative, agricultural and economic issues, the fight against the partisan movement, sending of prisoners of war to Germany, the mandatory supply of agricultural products, the taxation and collection of fines from the population. Information on the payment of tax by the residents of the village. Lists of prisoners of war who were on the territory of the village. Lists of employees of village administration, local police and population of village Levivka.

Collection includes order by Pidvysotsk district administration for the Jewish population to register (file 1).
