Baron Von Der Shpik | Two Friends

Soviet war songs recorded in Moscow, 1943. Stinson 3003. Side A: Baron von der Shpik [Baron von der Pshik]. Soviet anti-Hitler parody sung to the melody of the Jewish-American popular song "Bay mir bistu sheyn" [Bei Mir Bist Du Schon]. Text: Anatoly Fidrovsky Music: Sholom Secunda; arranged by Orest Kandat Vocals: Leonid Utesov State Jazz Orchestra of the USSR Side B: Two Friends [Dva Druga] Text: Vassily Lebedev-Kumach Music: Anatoly Lepin Vocals: Vadim Kozin Accordions: David Ashkenazi and A. Timofeev
