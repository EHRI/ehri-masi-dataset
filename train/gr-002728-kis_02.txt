Αρχείο του Οργανισμού Περιθάλψεως και Αποκαταστάσεως Ισραηλιτών Ελλάδος

192.900 pages.

O.P.A.I.E. was founded in 1949. It was succeeded Y.D.I.P. (Agency for the Custody of Jewish Property) which had supervised the expropriation and seizure of Jewish business, warehouses and properties.

Consists of several thematically arranged sections. Included are property restitution files, correspondence, inheritance documentation, files on individuals, and the like.
