Yiddish Intermediate School in Skuodas

Skuodo žydų vidurinė mokykla (Fond 796)

845 digital images, JPEG

Lietuvos centrinis valstybės archyvas

The Jewish community of Skuodas established and maintained the Jewish Secondary School of Skuodas in 1922. Skuodas Jewish Secondary School was a real pro-gymnasium type, later on, transformed into a four-grade co-educational school for boys and girls. The language of instruction was Hebrew.

Records of the Jewish Intermediate School in Skuodas. The collection contains minutes of meetings of the teachers’ council, records of the entrance examinations; requests from parents to admit their children to school; graduation certificates that include photos of students, class journals, etc.
