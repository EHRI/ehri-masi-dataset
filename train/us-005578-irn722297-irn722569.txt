Operation Annie - January 22, 1945

(NARA RG-165-62) In English, the announcer discusses where the fighting is happening between the SS and American, French, and British soldiers. Along with music, there is an eye-witness story about an air raid that is also told. There is also a report on the front. (NARA RG-165-63) In English, the announcer explains points of information that have been discussed in the last week, including the importance to keep sensitive documents top secret, evacuations happening in the west, the fighting occurring on the Eastern Front, issues with food supplies, and music.
