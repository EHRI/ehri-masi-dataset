John and Melanie Balzer papers

folder

1

The papers consist of a German police arrest warrant stating that Mirska Lina is to be held further in jail and a letter sent by the Reichs-Rundfunk-Gesellschaft (the National Socialist government's short-wave radio bureau) to Frederic Kueffner in Boston, Mass., describing a small Christmas gift that the organization is sending to German expatriates and listeners abroad.
