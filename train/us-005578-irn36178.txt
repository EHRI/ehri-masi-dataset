Bernhard Kullak papers

folder

1

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Consists of ten photographs of Bernhard Kullak, a Nazi physician. Includes photographs of Kullak in uniform treating a patient (at a field or training hospital). Includes some post-war pictures of Kullak and his wife, Friedel, as well as some pre-war images of a clothing store owned by Friedel's father, Emil Koch. Also includes Kullak's Ahnenpass, with genealogical information; family tree documents for Friedel Koch; and post-war copies of documents including Friedel's birth certificate, the couple's 1939 marriage certificate, and Bernhard Kullak's death certificate attesting to his 1943 death on the eastern front.
