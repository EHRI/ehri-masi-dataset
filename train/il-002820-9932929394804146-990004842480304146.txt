[Letter by the Jewish Central Information Office to Saly Mayer]

1 electronic ressource (1 page)

This file contains a letter written by the Jewish Central Information Office to Saly Mayer in 1936. They send him newspaper clips from the German newspaper "Welt-Dienst", which is owned by Fleischhauer, expert witness for the defense during the Bern trial. The clippings talk about the Bern trials as well as about their political antisemetic world view.
