"Memory Journey from Salonica to Paris"

Oral testimony of Janine Gerson-Père

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Isabelle Pleskoff, head librarian at the Musée d'art et d'histoire du judaïsme (Mahj), donated video recordings of Holocaust presentations and interviews produced by the Mahj to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 2015. The interview was conducted on June 1, 2014 and the original French title is "Itinéraire mémoriel de Salonique à Paris avec Janine Gerson-Père."
