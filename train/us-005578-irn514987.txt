Oral history interview with Antons Pirožoks

2 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. The interview was directed and supervised by Nathan Beyrak. The interview was conducted with Antons Pirožoks in Latvia for the Latvia Documentation Project on July 6, 2003. The interview was received by the Archives Branch in February 2004.
