Oral history interview with Sara Glutzman

2 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

The interview with Sara Glutzman was conducted on October 28, 1987 by Eli Pfefferkorn for the Krakow Underground Project. The project was coordinated by the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Council in Israel with Holocaust survivors who were members of the Krakow Underground.
