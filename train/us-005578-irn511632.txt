Oral history interview with Estelle Laughlin

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The interview with Estelle Laughlin was produced by Larry Papier as part of a project to interview Holocaust survivors in the Washington, D.C. area. This volunteer project was conducted with support and technical advice from the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the interview in 1992.
