Oral history interview with Frederic Bernard

3 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Linda Kuzmack, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, conducted the interview with Frederic Bernard on May 17, 1990 in Washington, DC. This interview is one of 51 oral histories included in the Miles Lerman Center for the Study of Jewish Resistance.
