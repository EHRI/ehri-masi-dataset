Correspondence with Central Jewish Lecture Committee (CJLC)

18 items

The Central Jewish Lecture Committee (CJLC) was a department of the Board of Deputies of British Jews tasked with coordinating education and publication related issues.

Correspondence regarding information and published material primarily related to the Nazi era including the reviewing of CJLC works by the Wiener Library, namely C. C. Aronsfeld. Beside letters the correspondence contains two annual surveys of the CJLC activities.
