Oral history interview with Hans-Henning Crome

1 digital files, MPEG-4

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. Wendy Lower, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, coordinated the interview with Hans-Henning Crome in Germany on December 3, 2010. The interview was received by the Museum's Oral History Branch in 2011.
