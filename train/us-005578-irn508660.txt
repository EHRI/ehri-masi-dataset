Oral history interview with Ruth (Renee) Hartz

2 sound cassettes (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Ruth (Renee) Hartz in Philadelphia, Pa., on February 28, 1982. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on September 22, 1998.
