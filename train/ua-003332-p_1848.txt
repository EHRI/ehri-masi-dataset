Editorial board of the "Sumy Herald" newspaper

8 files in 2 inventories

The collection contains requests from institutions to produce seals and stamps, their samples. Articles for publication, announcements, poems, cuttings from the newspaper "Sumy Herald" and from German newspapers. Information on wage billing for editorial staff.

The collection includes antisemitic articles published in the "Sumy Herald" newspaper under the German occupation (inv. 2, file 2, pp. 24-26, file 3, pp. 11, 14, 181).
