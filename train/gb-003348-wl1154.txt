Eric Walters-Kohn: Copy family documents

1 folder

Eric Walters-Kohn was born in Vienna in 1906, spent time in Dachau and Buchenwald, and came to Great Britain in 1939. Many family members died in the Holocaust including his mother.

This miscellaneous collection of copy papers document, in part, the life of Eric Walters-Kohn's family. The material comprises copy published and unpublished documents
