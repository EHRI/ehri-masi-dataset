Oral history interview with Norbert Krasnosielski

2 digital files, WAV

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Ina Navazelskis, Program Coordinator for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the audio interview with Norbert Krasnosielski on November 24, 2009. The video footage of the interview was recorded by Mr. Krasnosielski's grandson Allen Posacki.
