Munich Municipality: Documentation regarding the treatment of the Jews, 1933-1942

72 files

This Subsection is part of the collections compiled by the Central Historical Commission (CHC) of the Central Committee of Liberated Jews in the US Zone in Munich (Record Group M.1). The collection contains files concerning Jews, which the CHC received from the Munich Municipal Archives.

Documents dealing with the persecution of Jews during the Nazi period, mainly in Munich, but also in Frankfurt am Main, Ansbach and some of the other cities in Germany. The collection also contains the personal files of Jews from the Munich Welfare Department.
