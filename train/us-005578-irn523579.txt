Frank P. Herold (circa 1909-1990) was married to Eleanor Kirch Herold and had two children: John and Patricia. He enlisted in the United States Army in January 1944 and served in World War II.

The collection consists of 21 photographs taken in Bergen-Belsen and Ohrdruf concentration camps immediately following liberation in April 1945.
