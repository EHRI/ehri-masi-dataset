The Free American and Deutscher Weckruf und Beobachter, May 16, 1940.

The newspaper was donated to to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2006 by Philip Sherman.

Newspaper entitled The Free American and Deutscher Weckruf und Beobachter; dated May 16, 1940. The newspaper promoted German identity in the United States and sought to down play Nazism; in English; printed in the United States.
