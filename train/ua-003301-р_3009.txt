Kharkiv medico-legal mortuary of the Kharkiv city administration

95 files

Collection contains instructions of the Department of Health on the procedure for receiving patients, correspondence with city and district authorities on the restoration of lost metric statements, on the mass burial of corpses. Orders and acts of forensic medical examination. Register books. Lists of staff for obtaining rations.

Files 9-15, 17-20, 22-25, 27, 28, 55, 59 contain letters of transmittal about the dead individuals of Jewish origin to the mortuary stating their names, addresses, age and reason of death (suicide, starvation, sickness and so on).
