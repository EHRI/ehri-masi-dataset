Correspondence with Association of Jewish Ex-Servicemen and Women (AJEX)

22 letters

The Association of Jewish Ex-Servicemen and Women (AJEX) is a registered charity dedicated to the remembrance of Jewish men and women who died while serving in the British Armed Forces.

Correspondence regarding various issues including among others: information requests on West German military officers as well as the ambassador in London; the rearmament of West Germany; the state of the memorials at Buchenwald and other former concentration camp sites. In its response to the AJEX request on the latter the Library provides a detailed description of the state of the Bergen-Belsen memorial.
