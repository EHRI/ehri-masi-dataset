Oral history interview with Shari Weiss

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

University of Michigan-Dearborn

The University of Michigan conducted the interview with Shari Weiss on April 17, 1985. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Archives Branch received the tapes by transfer from the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch in February 1995.
