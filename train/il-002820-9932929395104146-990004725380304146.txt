Verzeichnis der bei der Propaganda-Abteilung vorrätigen Flugblätter, Handzettel und Plakate

1 electronic resource (1 page)

This file is a list of the leaflets, hand-outs and posters that are available at the propaganda department. There is no date mentioned. There are 10 leaflets listed and 3 picture hand-outs. Also the prices for ordering the material are given. Orders should be placed at the Main Branch Office of the National Socialist German Worker's Party, Schellinstr. 50, Munich 13.
