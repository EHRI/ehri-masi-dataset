Paula Albersheim: Personal papers

1 folder

Paula Albersheim was born in Nottuln in Westphalia, Germany on 12 December 1885. She emigrated to England in 1939. Her last known address before her death was in Dursley, Gloucestershire.

<p>This collection contains the personal papers of German Jew Paula Albersheim documenting her preparations for emigration to England in 1939.</p>
