Oral history interview with Joseph Kutrzeba

12 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Randy M. Goldman, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the oral history interview with Joseph Kutrzeba on May 18, 1995. This interview is one of 51 oral histories included in the Miles Lerman Center for the Study of Jewish Resistance.
