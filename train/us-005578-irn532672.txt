"Jim d'Etterbeek and the Holocaust"

Oral testimony of Jim Chaim Charles Kaliski

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Isabelle Pleskoff, head librarian at the Musée d'art et d'histoire du judaïsme (Mahj), donated video recordings of Holocaust presentations and interviews produced by the Mahj to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 2015. The interview was conducted on January 12, 2014 and the original French title is "Jim d'Etterbeek et le génocide avec Jim Chaim Charles Kaliski."
