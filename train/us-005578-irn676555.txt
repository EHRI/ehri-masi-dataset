Jewish Provincial Committee in Przemyśl

Wojewódzki Komitet Żydów w Przemyślu (Sygn.367)

2,348 digital images, PDF

Żydowski Instytut Historyczny im. Emanuela Ringelbluma

The Jewish Committee in Przemyśl was among the first committees established in Poland after the liberation of Polish territories by the Red Army in July 1944. For this reason, the collection contains very interesting documents about Polish Jews who survived the Holocaust.

Minutes of the committee meetings, reports, correspondence, lists of Jewish survivors, committee statutes, circulars, appeals and communications, budgets, lists of people using assistance, personal data of committee staff and some other documents..
