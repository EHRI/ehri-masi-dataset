Deutsche Arbeitsfront [German Labor Front] pin

overall: Height: 2.000 inches (5.08 cm) | Width: 2.000 inches (5.08 cm) | Depth: 0.375 inches (0.953 cm)

The Nazi Party pin was donated to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2005 by the University of Mary Washington Galleries.

Pin commemorating a 1934 march in the German city of Gera by the Deutsche Arbeitsfrong [German Labor Front.] The national labor union was established by the Nazi Party in 1933.
