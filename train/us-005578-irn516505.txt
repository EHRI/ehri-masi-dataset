Serviciul de Informaţii şi Securitate al Republicii Moldova

The Information and Security Service of the Republic of Moldova (Serviciul de Informații și Securitate al Republicii Moldova; SIS) is the principal intelligence agency of Moldova.

Contains records related to criminal investigations into war crimes and enemy collaboration in Moldova and Transnistria.
