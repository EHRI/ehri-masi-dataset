District polyclinic at Romny Board of health

5 files

Collection contains orders, decisions of the mayor of Romny, and of the head of health department on the education of the labor force, the fight against typhus, cleanliness of the city of Romny and the villages. Instructions for treating tetanus. Applications, certificates.

Collection includes order by the major of the town of Romny to register Jewish population not at town's Labor Registry Office but at the office of the Commissioner for the Jewish Labor (file 1, p. 1).
