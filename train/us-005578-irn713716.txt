Selected records of the District Commission to Investigate Nazi Crimes in Siedlce

Okręgowa Komisja Badania Zbrodni Niemieckich w Siedlcach (GK 180)

2,446 digital images, PDF

Instytut Pamięci Narodowej-Komisja Ścigania Zbrodni przeciwko Narodowi Polskiemu

Minutes, official correspondence, a study entitled "Losses of the Siedlce county during the Nazi occupation", a study about the Treblinka camp based on the investigation materials of the prosecutor Zdzisław Łukaszewicz, testimonies of witnesses and lists of those murdered in the Biała Podlaska, Łuków, Siedlce county, testimony of Piotr Litwiniusz on the mass execution of Jews in the Łomża Podlaska counties, materials on Soviet prisoner-of-war camps in Ostrówek, Kaliłów, Sielczyk, Suchożrebach and Wola Suchożerbska, camp for Italian and French prisoners of war in Siedlce, materials on war criminals in the counties of Biała Podlaska, Garwolin, Radzyń, and Siedlce. Files on germanization of Polish children and displacement from the Zamość region to the city and Siedlce county. Materials about the war graves of Belgian and Dutch citizens, questioning of witnesses about pseudo-medical experiences in KL Ravensbrück.
