Louis Drucker collection, 1945

folder

1

Louis Drucker served as a dental officer and grave registrar with the 67th Field Hospital at Wöbbelin concentration camp.

The collection consists of photographs taken by American Soldier Louis Drucker at the Wöbbelin concentration camp, Gardelegen, and Reims, France. Includes depictions of survivors, bodies, graves, German prisoners of war, and American and Soviet soldiers. Many of the photographs are annotated by Drucker on verso.
