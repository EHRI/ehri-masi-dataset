Erna Weyl and Walter Rothschild collection

boxes

14

Documents, photographs, pamphlets, books, and clippings concerning the experiences of Walter Rothschild and Erna Weyl Rothschild. Includes photo albums; loose prewar, wartime and postwar photographs; correspondence (some with translation); personal documents including citizenship papers, Army documents, birth, marriage, and death records; passports and identification cards; a US Army file including maps, photos, official documents, clippings, a menu from Camp Ritchie, and ID cards; and documents from the Holland America line.
