Oral history interview with Friedrich Kuntswald and Freya Kuntswald

1 digital files, MPEG-2

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. Wendy Lower, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, coordinated the interview with Friedrich Kuntswald and Freya Kuntswald in Germany on November 4, 2011. The interview was received by the Museum's Oral History Branch in 2011.
