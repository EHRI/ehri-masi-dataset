'Pogrom in Germany': flyer advertising protest

1 folder

The protest meeting to which this flyer refers took place some time in 1938 after <em>Reichskristallnacht</em>, at 'The Hippodrome', Golders Green. Among those attending were representatives from the Federation of Peace Councils, the Jewish People's Council and the Society of Friends.
