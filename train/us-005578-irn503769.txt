Heinz Bauer was born in Austria to Jewish parents, and he was a student at the University of Vienna during the Anschluss. After the Nazi seizure of power, Bauer and his family immigrated to Kenya via Paris and London. He later immigrated to the United States.

Contains information about the experiences of Heinz Bauer and his family as they emigrated to Kenya via Paris and London following the Anschluss. The memoir describes the years 1938-1940.
