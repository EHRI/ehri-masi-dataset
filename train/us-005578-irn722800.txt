Dized' ay trobadores

Dized' ay trobadores. Recorded in Madrid, 1993. Featuring Judith Cohen (voice, recorder, percussion) with assisting vocalists (Tamar Cohen Adams, Meme Eugenio, Pilar López, Alberto Alonso, Juan Manuel Rivero, Manuel Díez), and José Escudero (Galician bagpipes).
