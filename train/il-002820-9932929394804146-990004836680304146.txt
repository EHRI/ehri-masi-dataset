[Letter from Russian Orthodox Church on the subject of the Protocols of the Elders of Zion]

1 electronic resource (1 page)

Letter from the Russian Orthodox Church concerning the subject of the Protocols fo the Elders of Zion. The letter states that while the Church has no knowledge of the authenticity or lack of authenticity of the Protocols, and cannot provide an opinion concerning their authorship, it sees the practices and actions described in them as compatible with the Jewish religion and practices of the Jewish people.
