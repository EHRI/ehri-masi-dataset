Leopold Levy family papers

folders

7

Erica Levy (b.1931) grew up in Viersen, Germany. In 1938 she moved to Brussels, Belgium, with her sister, Karola, where she attended a convent school. She eventually reunited with her parents in London, England, in May 1940. The entire family emigrated to the United States in Sept. 1940.

Contains correspondence, photographs, lists, and various other documents relating to the Leopold Levy family in the 1930s and 1940s. Includes letters to the American Consulate in Geneva, Switzerland, relating to employment for refugees, the immigration of Erica Levy as a child, and a personal letter describing the fate of other Levy family members unable to escape Germany before the Holocaust.
