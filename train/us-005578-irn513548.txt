Oral history interview with Perla Sussi

3 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Jasa Almuli, project director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Perla Sussi on November 12, 1996. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in December 1996.
