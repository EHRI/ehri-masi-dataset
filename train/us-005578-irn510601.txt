Oral history interview with Sara Yakovlena Kasperskaya

2 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak, project director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Sara Kasperskaya on August 11, 1995. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in September 1995.
