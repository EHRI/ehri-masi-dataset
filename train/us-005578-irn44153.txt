United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Consists of documents, passports, correspondence, newspaper articles regarding the life of Dr. Heinrich Bund (later Henry Bund), originally of Vienna, Austria. Includes information about his education in Austria, emigration to the United States in 1938, pre-war, wartime, and post-war employment, and his extensive efforts to gain ownership and restitution for family property in Poland and in Vienna. Also includes information about his mother, Dorothea Bund (later Bond) and brother, Eduard Bund (later Edward Bond) both of whom also emigrated to the United States in 1938. Also includes an English-language translation of Heinrich Bund's 1938 doctoral thesis, "Die Kapitalanlangegesellschaft" [The Capital Investment Company] along with an explanation of the contents, which argued against Nazi economic policy.
