Oral history interview with Eduards Smelters

2 videocassettes (DVCAM), sound, color ; 1/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. The interview was directed and supervised by Nathan Beyrak. The interview was conducted with Eduards Smelters on January 6, 2007 for the Museum's Latvia Documentation Project. The Oral History Branch received the interview in August 2007.
