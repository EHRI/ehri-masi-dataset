Oral history interview with Abraham H. Biderman

3 videocassettes (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Jewish Holocaust Museum and Research Centre (Melbourne, Vic.)

The Jewish Holocaust Museum and Research Centre conducted the interview on February 27, 1997, March 13, 1997, and April 20, 1997, in Melbourne, Australia. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview in August 1999.
