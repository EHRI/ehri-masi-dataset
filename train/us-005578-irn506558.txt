Oral history interview with Alice Schiffer

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Gay Block

Gay Block and Malka Drucker produced the interview with Alice Schiffer on May 18, 1988 for their book "Rescuers: Portraits of Moral Courage in the Holocaust," Holmes & Meier, 1992, as well as their documentary film "They Risked Their Lives: Rescuers of the Holocaust." The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch received the tapes of the interviews from Malka Drucker and Gay Block in March 1989.
