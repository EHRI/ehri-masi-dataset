Allgemeine Wirtschaftsgeographie

1 electronic resource (34 pages)

The file contains a research paper regarding the general economic geography, in particular the economic human and the environment. It describes and explains economic areas of the earth's surface after its economic structures, as well as the processes and functions of producing them. The author says, that it is hard to define economic geography as it is important to understand and seperate the diverse occurrence of people, nations, tribes, religions and beliefs for its understanding. Furthermore the political systems and their approach to economy is a factor with a great value. In his research paper he tries to find a way to take all the pieces into consideration to show its problematic and its prospects.
