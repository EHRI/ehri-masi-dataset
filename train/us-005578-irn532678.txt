"Saved by a Right Man in Vilnius"

Oral testimony of Simon Malkès

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Isabelle Pleskoff, head librarian at the Musée d'art et d'histoire du judaïsme (Mahj), donated video recordings of Holocaust presentations and interviews produced by the Mahj to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 2015. The interview was conducted on December 14, 2014 and the original French title is "Sauvés par un Juste à Vilnius avec Simon Malkès."
