Presentation by Kenneth Rosenbaum Goldsmith

2 videocassettes (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Kenneth Rosenbaum Goldsmith (Kurt Simon Rosenbaum) donated the recording of his presentation to his grandson Jason Rosenbaum Goldsmith's fifth grade class to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in July 2015. The presentation was recorded at Nancy Ryles Elementary School in Beaverton, Oregon in April 1994.
