Oral history interview with Ina Rennert-Rakavy

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Association Memorie et Documents

Association Memorie et Documents conducted the interview with Ina Rennert-Rakavy on December 20, 1993. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tape of the interview from the Association Memorie et Documents on June 11, 1996.
