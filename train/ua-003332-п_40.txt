Konotop city committee of the Communist party of Ukraine

9841 files

Collection contains minutes of conferences, plenums, meetings of the bureau, meetings of party and economic officials, city-wide meetings of secretaries of party organizations, heads of enterprises and materials to them. Plans, work reports. Information, memos, acts, correspondence on the implementation of the decisions of the highest bodies on issues of organizational, propaganda, social and economic development. Materials and correspondence about the underground activities of the sabotage group at the steam locomotive plant in 1942-1943. Acts of damage inflicted by the German invaders during the Great Patriotic War.

Collection includes statements and acts about atrocities committed by the German occupiers regarding the Jewish population (inventory 1, file 5, pp. 21-22).
