[Nazi Germany and post-war Europe]

1 electronic resource (117 pages)

The file contains several press cuttings from different newspapers and countries. Some articles are regarding the Nazi regime and the reaction of other countries, others are regarding the Soviet Union. The topics also differ from each other. Some are regarding Germans, who want to buy real estate in Holland, others shortage of medicine and hospital space, deportation of nurses from Belgium to Germany or political development. The variety of articles is huge, but all of them concerning the Nazi regime and politics in post war Europe. Like the division of tasks in the post war European economy, where the division of labour was planned in such a way, that it was spoken about a Western district, including Holland, Belgium and France, a North Baltic district, including amongst others Germany and Denmark, the South Eastern district, which centers around Vienna and the Eastern district.
