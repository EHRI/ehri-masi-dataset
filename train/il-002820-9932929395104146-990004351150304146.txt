[Treatment regarding racial desirable children of foreigners]

1 electronic resource (3 Pages)

The file contains correspondence regarding racial desirable children of families with only one german parent. The first case is about a child of a Ukrainian woman and a german man. Its suggested in the letter to hurry the racial screening because of the age of the child it might be troublesome to part it from the parents. The second case is about a pregnant Polish woman whose request for an abortion was denied since she is carrying a racial desirable child that is supposed to go to a Lebensborn home. The german original of the document is added.
