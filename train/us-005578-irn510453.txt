Oral history interview with Helen Abramovitz

1 sound cassette (90 min.),

Special Collections and Archives, Paul Laurence Dunbar Library, Wright State University

Wright State University conducted the interview with Helen Abramovitz on July 30, 1984 in preparation for the program, "Faces of the Holocaust." The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received a copy of the interview on July 7, 1995.
