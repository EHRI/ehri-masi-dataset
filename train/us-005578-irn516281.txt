Arthur Ruden was in the 4th Armored Division of the United States Army that liberated Ohrdruf and Buchenwald concentration camps

The collection consists of three photographs taken at the time of liberation at Buchenwald concentration camp in Germany. One of the photographs depicts American Generals Eisenhower, Patton, and Walker at Buchenwald. Captions in English on the verso.
