Helena Piasecka collection

folder

1

Contains copies of photographs and documents, and copies of newspaper clippings related to the Holocaust experiences of Helena Piasecka, originally of Zuromir, Poland. Mrs. Piasecka, a member of the Polish underground, was a victim of medical experiments in Ravensbrück (these female victims were also called "Lapins") and was a witness for the British in the IMT (Nuremberg) medical trials. Includes copies of photographs, of her personal documents, and of clippings and articles related to Helena Piasecka and the other Ravensbrück "Lapins."
