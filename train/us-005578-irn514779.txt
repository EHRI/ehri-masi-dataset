Wlodawa resistance fighters photographs

folder

1

Consists of wartime and post-war photographs from the collection of Rachel Ejber Birnbaum. The majority of the photographs were taken in the Włodawa, Poland, ghetto and depict members of the resistance, including Ajzik Ejber and Jacob Ejber, who later perished in Adampol. Also includes post-war photographs of people who returned to Włodawa after the war and a photograph of piles of grain on the floor of a synagogue in Włodawa which the Germans had turned into a grainery.
