Deutschland und der Weltfrieden

1 electronic resource (13 pages)

The file contains a report regarding the book of Sven Hedin, who was a Swedish geographer, topographer, explorer, photographer, travel writer, and illustrator of his own works. The Jewish Central Information Office published and distribute a report based on the information from the book of Hedin, furthermore they tried to advertise his book. In 1937 Hedin refused to publish his book "Deutschland und der Weltfrieden" (Germany and World Peace) in Germany because the Reich Ministry for Public Enlightenment and Propaganda insisted on the deletion of Nazi-critical passages. But in this report it can be read that the propaganda machine in Germany was already using his criticism on the Nazi regime and praised his book as an extraordinary work of an international worker.
