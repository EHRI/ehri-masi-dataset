Fred Feld papers

folder

1

The Fred Feld papers consists of a passport ("Reisepass") stamped with a " J" issued to Mathias Scheer [Fred Feld's uncle] in Austria on December 1, 1938, and two postcards written by Baruch Wiener in Tarnów, Poland, to his brother-in-law, Mathias Scheer in Brooklyn , N.Y. in September 1941.
