Oral history interview with Ruth Rontal

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois

The Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois (now Illinois Holocaust Museum and Education Center) conducted the interview with Ruth Rontal on July 31, 1994. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History branch received the tape of the interview from the Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois on June 20, 1995.
