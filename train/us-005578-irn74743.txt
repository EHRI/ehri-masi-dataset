Oral history interview with Isaac Mischkinsky and Bella Mischkinsky

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Peter Lerner donated a copy of his oral history interview with Isaac Mischkinsky and Bella Mischkinsky to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in June 2013. The interview was recorded circa 1975 in Beechhurst, Queens, NY.
