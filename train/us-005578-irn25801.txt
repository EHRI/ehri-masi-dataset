Box of slides depicting propaganda of Nazi Germany and Europe

Height: 6.500 inches (16.51 cm) | Width: 2.130 inches (5.41 cm) | Depth: 216.500 inches (549.91 cm)

The box of slides were donated to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 1995 by Mortimer Schaffer.

Obtained by an American G.I. in a home during the final stages of the war in Europe.
