Oral history interview with James Hirsch

3 videocassettes (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Dallas Holocaust Museum, Center for Education and Tolerance

James Pennebaker and Alan Griffin of the Dallas Memorial Center for Holocaust Studies conducted the interview with James Hirsch on October 21, 1991. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History branch received the tapes of the interview from the Dallas Memorial Center for Holocaust Studies on July 10, 1992. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the interview by transfer from the Oral History branch in February 1995.
