Oral history interview with Marian Filar

3 sound cassettes (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Marian Filar in Philadelphia, Pa., on September 5 and November 16, 1994 and June 16, 1995. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on September 22, 1998.
