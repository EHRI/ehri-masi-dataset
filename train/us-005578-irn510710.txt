Oral history interview with Felicia Weingarten

1 sound cassette (90 min.),

Jewish Community Relations Council, Anti-Defamation League of Minnesota and the Dakotas

The interview with Felicia Weingarten was conducted by Rhoda G. Lewin on June 2, 1983, as part of a Holocaust oral history project sponsored by the Jewish Community Relations Council, Anti-Defamation League of Minnesota and the Dakotas. The interview was acquired by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 1992.
