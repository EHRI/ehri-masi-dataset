Oral history interview with Shalom Eilati

15 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak conducted the interview with Shalom Eilati in Israel on February 14, 1999. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview on June 10, 1999, as an accretion to the original collection of Israel Documentation Project interviews received by transfer in February 1995.
