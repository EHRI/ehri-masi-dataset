Herzberg family papers

folders

5

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Contains photographs, correspondence, and official documents related to the family of Jacob and Margaret (nee Bloch) Herzberg, and their daughter Rachel, originally of Fulda, Germany. Includes family photographs and documents, including birth certificates, certificates related to religious ceremonies and weddings, extending back to late 1890s. Also included are forms and correspondence related to the efforts of the Herzberg family to emigrate from Germany, their successful arrival in the United States, Jacob Herzberg's application to not be considered as an enemy alien, and U.S. naturalization certificates.
