Nazi Welfare Org'n: 10 year report, community assistance

Title: "Ein Leistungsbericht zum 10 jährigen bestehen der NSV." [A progress report on 10 years of achievement by the Nazi Welfare Organization]. 1932: Man stamping ID papers, pan, factories with smokestacks. "Eröffnung des Winterhilfswerkes 1933/34". Headlines of Voelkischer Beobachter. Nazi community self-help - Winterhelp work (WHW). Collecting, donating money. Crowds, street entertainment, circus, airplane, marching band in streets, gymnastics, exchanging money, officials meet. CU, "Mutter und Kind" card. Donating supplies of food. Women sewing, children's shoes. "Winterhilf Werk 1933/34" written on back of truck, guard. Maps, animation: "Kleider-Verteilung" ; Pfundespende" ; "Kartoffel-Versorgung" Trains, banners reading Winterhelfewerk. Men with sacks, donating supplies and food. Huge vats of soup, large dining halls, band playing outdoors, Hitler donating money, soup. More animation: "Eintopf" (large soup bowl) ; "Sachspende-gemuse" (ships) ; "Fleisch" (animals) ; "Seefische" (fishing) ; "Mehl" ; "Zucker" ; "Kohlen." 04:30:47 "Ein Volk, ein Reich, ein Fuehrer" Troops crossing Austrian border, marching. Food, clothing, rations. Walking in streets with belongings. HJ playing drums. Children playing, receiving mail. Toddlers in school, nursery. Relaxing, eating. Cannons firing. Crowded streets, receiving food from truck. Producing ornaments. CUs, Christmas ornaments with "WHW" and Santa, from 1934, 1935, 1937, 1939, etc. Animated map. "Weihnachten in jedem Deutschenhaus - Wir helfen!" pin.
