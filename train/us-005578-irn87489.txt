Moskovits Archive: Court cases against Dierig Holding Company

Archivo Moskovits: Prozess gegen Firma Dierig Holding

2029 digital images, JPEG

2 microfilm reels, 35 mm

Yiṿo in Argenṭine

Legal cases and correspondence related to the trial against Dierig Holding company that used forced and slave labor during Nazi era. It consist of lawsuits of Holocaust survivors and former prisoners of Nazi concentration camps against Dierig Holding company who worked as forced labors at company's branches in Nazi Germany. The trial took place in 1999.
