Sudbeni stol u Mostaru

77 boxes, 8 books

The court was active from 1941 to 1945. Iz had juidicial authority in Mostar, Prozor, Lištica, Konjic, Ljubinje, Ljubuški and Stolac. Since 1st of November 1941. it also supervised local court in Trebinje, Nevesinje, Gacko, Bileća and Ravno.
It mainly handled criminal cases as second-level, regional court, but it also handled complaints, declaring of deceased or missing persons during the war, sales of property, etc.
It was abolished on 3rd of February 1945.
