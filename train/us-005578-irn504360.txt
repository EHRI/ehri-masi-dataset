Selected records from the National Archives of Moldova

57,517 digital images, JPEG

24 microfilm reels (digitized), 35 mm

Archiva Naţională a Republicii Moldovei

Various documents relating to internment of Jews in several ghettos in Romania and Bessarabia; administration of the Chișinău ghetto (including census information); the ghetto in Bălți ; the ghetto in Soroca; activities of the police in the Chișinău ghetto; disposal of Jewish property; deportations of Jews from Bessarabia to Transnistria (Ukraine); and executions of Romanian Jews.
