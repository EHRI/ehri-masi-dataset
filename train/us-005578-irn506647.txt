Oral History interview with Emilie Klugmann Szekely

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received this oral history interview from Emilie Szekely on March 25, 1999. Emilie Szekely sent the correct copy of the interview to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives on April 16, 1999.
