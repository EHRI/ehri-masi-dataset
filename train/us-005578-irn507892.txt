Oral history interview with Anna Świętojańska

1 videocasette (Betacam SP),

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. The interview was directed and supervised by Nathan Beyrak. Michal Sobelman and Michael Tregenza conducted the interview with Anna Świętojańska in Belzec, Poland on March 13, 1998, for the Polish Witnesses to the Holocaust Project.The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tape of the interview on November 13, 1998.
