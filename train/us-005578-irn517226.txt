The collection consists of two black and white photographs: an image of four children; verso: faded blue ink inscriptions, "Riva/Bekker" [donor] handwritten in black ink; dated 1947; Mohyliv-Podilʹsʹkyĭ, Ukraine, and an image of a baby seated on chair, boy standing next to chair with one hand on baby's back, purple ink inscription, "Riva/Bekker" [donor], handwritten in black ink; dated June 1937; Mohyliv-Podilʹsʹkyĭ, Ukraine.
