Kahn family: papers

1 folder

Seligmann Kahn was a manufacturer from Heilbronn, Germany. He married Alice Levi in 1919. They had three children: Edith (born in 1920), Annemarie (born in 1923) and Walter (born in 1926).

This collection contains the papers and photographs of Seligmann and Alice Kahn, a Jewish family from Heilbronn.
