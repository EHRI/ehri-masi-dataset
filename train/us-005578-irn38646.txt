Bauchwitz family collection

folder

1

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Consists of information regarding the Holocaust experiences of siblings Peter Siegbert Bauchwitz and Rita Bauchwitz Molony, both of Halle, Germany. Includes biographical and memoir information, in German and English, about Peter Bauchwitz, copies of correspondence regarding the Jewish community of Halle, and copies of official wartime documentation and correspondence related to the Bauchwitz family (focused on father Paul Bauchwitz and on Rita Bauchwitz). Includes lists of property looted from the family. Rita and Peter were both able to immigrate to Palestine, but their parents were deported from Halle in 1942 and perished in the Holocaust.
