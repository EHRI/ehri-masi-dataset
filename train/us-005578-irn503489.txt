Oudegeest, William

William and Mies Oudegeest (husband and wife) are natives of Holland and were participants in underground movements during the Nazi occupation of the Netherlands. After immigration to the United States they settled in Little Rock, Arkansas.

Consists of transcripts of an audiotape interview of William and Mies Oudegeest concerning their participation in underground resistance during the Nazi occupation of the Netherlands and their hiding of Dutch Jews. Also included are the audiotapes containing the Oudegeest interview, and copies of a letter in Dutch, with English translation.
