Minutes of the meeting held in Paris, on June 13th, 1939

1 electronic resource (24 pages)

The file contains minutes of the meeting held in Paris, on June 13th, 1939, released by the 'Liaison committee of the high commissioner for refugees under the protection of the league of Nations'. Representatives of several organizations were participating in the meeting. The agenda was concerning the change of the status of the committee, ratification of the minutes of the last meeting and the admission of new organizations to membership. Besides the participants were dicussing about the situation and the continuing proceeding with Jewish refugees from Czechoslovakia and from Bulgaria. Furthermore reports on the question of professional training and on the financial resources of those privat organizations which finance refugee work have been presented. At the end of the meeting further annotations, questions and ideas have been discussed.
