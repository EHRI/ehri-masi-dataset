[Deutschfeindliche Propaganda in der Türkei]

1 electronic resource (5 pages)

The file contains a report of the German Embassy of Turkey to the German Foreign Ministry in Berlin dated 04.06.1933, signed by German Ambassador [Wilhelm] Fabricius, regarding anti –German agitation and propaganda in Turkey. It evaluates the Turkish Press and Radio and the influences of the French, Polish and Russian Embassies in Turkey.
