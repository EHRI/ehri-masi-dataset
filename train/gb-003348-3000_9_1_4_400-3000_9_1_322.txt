Correspondence with Deutsche Universitätszeitung

19 letters

Founded in 1945 the (DUZ) is a fortnighty journal concerning German universities and science.

The correspondence with various correspondents regard the exchange of published material, mainly issues of the DUZ and the Library’s Bulletin. Moreover, two copies of reports by journalist and former DUZ employee Hans Geert Falkenberg on the Nazi era are contained. They concern his persecution as ('mixed-blood') as well the life of his grandmother and her murder during the Holocaust.
