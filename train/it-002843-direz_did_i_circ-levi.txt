Registro 137 F (1936-37)

Approximately 200 pages

School register of female students of public Mantua schools, including also Luisa Levi, a Jewish student born in Mantua on November, 10th, 1929, from Samuele and Elide Levi. 
She will be the youngest among the Mantuan Jews deported to the concentration camps (on April, 5th, 1944); she will later die in Bergen-Belsen.
