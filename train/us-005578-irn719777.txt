Oral history interview with anonymous interviewee 237

Dr. Joanna Tokarska-Bakir donated the audio recordings and transcripts of the Ethnographic Archive to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in April 2018. Dr. Tokarska-Bakir headed the interview project as part of the Institute for Applied Social Sciences, University of Warsaw and Institute of Slavic Studies of the Polish Academy of Sciences from 2004-2012. This interview was conducted in the Sandomierz region of Poland.
