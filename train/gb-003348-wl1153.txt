Copy documents re Bad Aussee resistance movement and Operation Bernhard

1 folder

The documents are copies from the <em>Dokumentationsarchiv des Oesterreichischen Widerstandes</em>. The original archival reference number (where known) has been repeated with the descriptions. Whatever details about the provenance of the originals are known have been added to the descriptions.

This collection of copy documentation relates to the Austrian resistance movement during the Nazi era and to the attempt by the Nazis to wage economic warfare by flooding Britain with counterfeit British currency in the operation named <em>Unternehmen Bernhard</em>, after the Gestapo officer in charge, Bernhard Krueger
