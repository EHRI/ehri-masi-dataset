Oral history interview with Marie Fauss

2 videocassettes (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Dallas Holocaust Museum, Center for Education and Tolerance

James Pennebaker and Alan Griffin of the Dallas Memorial Center for Holocaust Studies conducted the interview with Marie Fauss on January 19, 1986. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History branch received the tapes of the interview from the Dallas Memorial Center for Holocaust Studies on November 28, 1991. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the interview by transfer from the Oral History branch in February 1995.
