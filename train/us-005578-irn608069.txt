Archiwum Państwowe w Warszawie Oddział w Grodzisku Mazowieckim

Municipality Guzów-the former rural municipality existed until 1954 year in the province. Warsaw in Poland. In the interwar period it belonged to the municipality Guzów Błoński, county in the province.Warsaw. Currently most of its former area belongs mainly in the municipality of Wiskitki .

Book inventory of the municipality Guzów in Poland: Contains the inventory of property and financial spending of commune Guzów, 1928-1936.
