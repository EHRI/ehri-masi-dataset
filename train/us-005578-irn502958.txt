Fojn, Anton

Consists of copies of name lists and other documents relating to Roma children living in the St. Josefspflege at Mulfingen, Germany, and their deportation to Auschwitz. Also included is a copy of a 1984 article by Johannes Meister titled "Schicksale der 'Zigeunerkinder' aus der St. Josefspflege in Mulfingen." The essay concerns the removal of Roma children from the St. Josefspflege orphanage and their deportation to camps where they were involved in "race hygiene" research conducted by Dr. Robert Ritter.
