Oral history interview with Ephraim Glaser

1 sound cassette (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Ephraim Glaser in Haifa, Israel, on August 10, 1988. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on September 30, 1999.
