James H. Mahoney was born in 1918. He graduated from Northwestern University medical school and served in the US army until 1946. While in the military, he was a member of the 120th Evacuation Hospital and provided medical treatment to liberated prisioners of Buchenwald concentration camp

Collection of 27 photographs of images of Buchenwald concentration camp immediately after its liberation.
