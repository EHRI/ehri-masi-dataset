Correspondence with Bernfes, Alexander B.

6 letters

Alexander Bernfes (1909-1986) was a Polish born British Holocaust survivor. After having escaped the Warsaw Ghetto in 1941 he came to England and built up a large collection of Holocaust related films, photographs and other testimonies of German atrocities.

Correspondence regarding archival material on the Warsaw Ghetto, and a potential involvement of The Wiener Library in an upcoming exhibition on this subject.
