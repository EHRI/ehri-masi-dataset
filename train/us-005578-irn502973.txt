Oral history interview with Ya'akov Tzur

3 videocassettes (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak conducted the interview with Ya'akov Tsur in Israel on April 21, 1994. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview on March 1, 1995, as an accretion to the original collection of Israel Documentation Project interviews received by transfer in February 1995.
