Neues Leben: Miscellaneous papers

1 folder

Nothing is known about the provenance of these documents.

<em>Neues Leben</em>, was a club devoted to nudism, founded in February 1930. On 3 March 1933 Goering issued a decree against the nudist culture. The movement had been non-political but by the end of March1933, following difficulties, avowed publicly their attachment to Hitler. A meeting of 19 July 1933 adopted a change of name to&nbsp;<em>Bund fuer aufartende Lebensfuehrung und Nordische Sittenklarheit</em>&nbsp;

<p>Papers of the organization Neues Leben including a membership list, 1934; article on its foundation; and miscellaneous papers</p>
