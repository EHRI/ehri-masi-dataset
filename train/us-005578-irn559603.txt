Selected records of the town Ostrowiec Świętokrzyski

Akta miasta Ostrowca Świętokrzyskiego (Sygn. 2891) : Wybrane materialy

10,748 digital images, PDF

Archiwum Państwowe w Kielcach

Selected records pertaining to personal data of inhabitants of Ostrowiec Świętokrzyski as well as a suburb Denków, including: the register books of permanent inhabitants, alphabetic lists of electors with the right to vote in 1939, and birth, death, and marriage registries from the Synagogue of Ostrowiec Świętokrzyski.
