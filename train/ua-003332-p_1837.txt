Bureau of the House management No. 8 of the Sumy city administration

59 files

Collection contains house-register books, lists of workers in house management and tenants. Inventory information, orders for apartments. Information on the payment of wages.

Collection includes house-registers of Sumy dwellers during the German occupation, including the Jewish dwellers (file 12, p. 6, file 31, p. 5, file 35, pp. 32-34).
