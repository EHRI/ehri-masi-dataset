Die Poiliche Juden | Lomir Sich Iberbeiten

Early 20th century reflection on the Jewish situation in the Russian Empire (A) and folk song (B) recorded in New York in 1922. Side A, Track 1, Die Poiliche Juden (Di poylishe yidn) Words and music: Mark Warshawsky Vocalist: Isa Kremer Piano: Kurt Hetzel Side B, Track 1, Lomir Sich Iberbeiten (Lomir zikh iberbetn) Vocalist: Isa Kremer Piano: Kurt Hetzel
