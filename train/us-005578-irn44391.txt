Oral history interview with Khosrow Banayan

1 digital file, WAV

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Peggy Frankston, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the interview with Dr. Khosrow Asher Banayan in Bordeaux, France on September 8, 2011. This interview is part of a series of USHMM-produced interviews about Jewish life in countries in North Africa during the Holocaust.
