Oral history interview with Helmut Kobler

2 videocassettes (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Helmut Kobler on August 13, 1992, November 23, 1992, February 11, 1993, June 17, 1993, September 9, 1993, and March 11, 1994. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in October 2000.
