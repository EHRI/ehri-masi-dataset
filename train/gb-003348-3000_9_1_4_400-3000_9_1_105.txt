Correspondence with Barber, Stephen S.

5 letters

Dr. Stephen S. Barber was a German speaking B’nai B’rith Lodge London member.

Correspondence regarding a book about Theresienstadt (Terezin), and a pondered research project on Czechoslovakian Jews, supported by the Claims Conference. Contained is a not related letter to a German law firm referring to the restitution claims of a woman who had been a forced labourer for the Hugo Schneider AG (HASAG) in Częstochowa.
