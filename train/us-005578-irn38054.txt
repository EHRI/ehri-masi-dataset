Oral history interview with Fred Jarvis

5 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Peggy Frankston, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, in association with the Musée Mémorial du Camp de Rivesaltes, conducted the interview with Fred Jarvis on June 9, 2009. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch received the interview in June 2009.
