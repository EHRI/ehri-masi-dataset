Lisa Kouchelevitz Rosenblum letter

folder

1

Lisa Rosenblum was arrested and imprisoned at Drancy by the Vichy police on 07 Oct. 1943. She was deported to Auschwitz on 28 Oct. 1943 via convoy number 61. She perished while in the camp.

Consists of a letter in Yiddish (one page with text on both sides), written by Lisa Kouchelevitz Rosenblum during her deportation from Drancy to Auschwitz on convoy number 61 in October 1943.
