Oral history interview with Helen Farkas

1 videocassette (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Helen Farkas on September 13, 1990, November 14, 1990, and December 20, 1990. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in October 2002.
