Hilde Walter: experiences of the Third Reich

1 folder

The report covers, amongst other things, the following subjects: the author's experience as a journalist on newspapers published by Rudolf Mosse Verlag; the publication and editing of <em>Weltbühne</em>; activities of the Berlin-Wilmersdorf branch of the SPD; membership of the Soroptimist Club, international association for professional women; administration and tenancy of the so-called Künstlerkolonie.
