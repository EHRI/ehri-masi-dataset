Refugee camps in Switzerland: Various records

c340 frames

The provenance of this collection is unknown.

<p><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>Readers need to reserve a terminal in the reading room to access a digital version of this collection.</strong></span></p><p>This microfilm collection of copy records documents the official policy regarding the management of refugees in Switzerland and the day to day running of refugee camps. The papers consist of memoranda, circulars and minutes of meetings of camp leaders.</p><p>Copies of instructions issued by the Eidgenossisches Justiz- und Polizeidepartment, Polizeiabteilung, Arbeitslager für Emigranten including:<br /><br />Miscellaneous memoranda, 24 Apr 1940- 10 Apr 1942, 14 pp, including 'Reglement für die Lagerführung, Provisorisch, Oct 1940, 3pp<br /><br />Rundschreiben Nos. 10, 11, 13-38, 40-78, 80, 107-110, 152, 17 May 1940- 27 Dec 1943<br />Mitteilungen an die Lagerleitung, Nos. 80, 88, 118, 17 Dec 1943-17 May 1944<br /><br />Minutes of the periodic meetings of the 'Lagerleiter' and 'Hilfsleiter' 7 Feb, 19 May, 6-8 Aug 1941, 18 Apr, 19-20 Jun 1942, 3-5 and 14-16 Aug 1944, 14-17 Feb 1945, pp146<br /><br />Article: "Was kann die Schweiz über die Lager den Emigranten geben", by Friedrich Süss, nd, pp 12<br /><br />"Reise nach Frankreich", by R. Kagi-Fuchsmann, 1 Aug 1945, 7 pp<br /></p>
