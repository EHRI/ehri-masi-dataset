US Army infantry advances in Germany

Staff Film Report no. 46; Combat Bulletin, no. 46. (with intertitles) Reel 2: The 29th Division crosses bridge at Juelich. Aerial views of the wrecked city of Juelich. Gen. Gerhardt and his G-2 officer report plans and conditions at a staff meeting. Troops cross a bridge under enemy fire. Gen. Gerhardt crosses the bridge. The 102nd Division liberates US prisoners at Erkelenz. Undelivered Red Cross packages are found. The 1st Army battles toward Cologne; the 83d Division takes Neusse. Generals Eisenhower, Simpson, and McLain visit 9th Army lines. 08:36:23 "India-Burma - Activities in Burma" Japanese mines are removed in Burma.
