Folk Songs from the Warsaw Ghetto

https://collections.ushmm.org/oh_findingaids/RG-50.060.0067_sum_en.pdf

Folk song from the Warsaw Ghetto: conceived in 1943, it describes families being torn apart at the Umschlagplatz, knowing that once transported to Treblinka, they would never return. Sung by Frieda Radasky, a survivor of the Warsaw Ghetto, and camps.
