Hadassa Cudzynowski was born in 1926 to Bezalel and Sara Cudzynowski and, in 1943, was taken as forced labor to the FaulbruÌˆck labor camp and later to the Langenbilen labor camp, where she was liberated. Her parents and sister Lola were deported to Auschwitz in 1943, where her parents perished.

Consists of 23 pre-war and wartime photographs of the family of Hadassa Cudzynowski, of Sosnowiec, Poland. Many members of the family, including many pictured, perished during the Holocaust.
