Correspondence, documents, and related materials concerning Kurt Goldmann, pertaining to his life in Germany from his birth in 1921 through his emigration in 1939; his experiences as a newly arrived immigrant to the U.S. as a student at Penn State University and membership in the Phi Sigma Delta fraternity; his Army service during WWII; and post-war experiences as a naturalized American. Includes materials pertaining to his parents Paul and Hedwig (Hede) Goldmann and their emigration from Germany to England and then to the U.S., including family records created prior to and during the Nazi era, personal papers, and commercial documents from the family business, some of which date to the late nineteenth century.
