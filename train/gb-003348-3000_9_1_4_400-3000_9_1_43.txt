Correspondence with Adler, Jakob

3 letters

Jakob Adler was a Jewish-German businessman. Based in Nuremberg, he had emigrated when the Nazis came to power. After the Second World war he worked temporarily for the Italian ministry of economics.

Correspondence regarding a restitution related information request by Adler on the Nazi Boycott of Jewish businesses from April 1, 1933. Contained is a brief personal account of this event.
