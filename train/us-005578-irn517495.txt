Oral history interview with Stanislav Girdo

2 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. The interview was directed and supervised by Nathan Beyrak. The interview was conducted with Stanislav Girdo in Latvia for the Latvia Documentation Project on February 28, 2004. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in May 2005.
