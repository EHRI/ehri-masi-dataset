Oral history interview with Rachelle Perahia Margosh

6 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch conducted the interview with Rachelle Margosh (b. Perahia) on June 24, 2004. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives in June 2004.
