Oral history interview with Richard Darr

1 sound cassette (90 min.),

Jewish Community Relations Council, Anti-Defamation League of Minnesota and the Dakotas

The interview with Richard Darr was conducted by Rhoda G. Lewin on June 13, 1984 as part of a Holocaust oral history project sponsored by the Jewish Community Relations Council, Anti-Defamation League of Minnesota and the Dakotas. The interview was acquired by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 1992.
