Photographic portrait of Dora Lichtensztein (nee Olszer; donor's aunt) from Łódź, Poland; stamped at lower right corner with text that reads "G. KANP. SNIERNIEWICE." Dora lived in the Łódź ghetto and was deported to Auschwitz concentration camp with her husband Marek and daughter Marysia, where they died together.
