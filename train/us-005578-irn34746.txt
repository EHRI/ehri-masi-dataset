Oral history interview with Haim-Vidal Sephiha

2 videocassettes (DVCAM), sound, color ; 1/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch conducted the interview with Haim-Vidal Sephiha in Washington, DC, on December 21, 2007. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in January 2008.
