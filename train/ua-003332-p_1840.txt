Bureau of the House management No. 11 of the Sumy city administration

45 files

Collection contains house-register books, lists of workers in house management and tenants. Inventory information, orders for apartments. Information on the payment of wages.

Collection includes house-registers of Sumy dwellers during the German occupation, including the Jewish dwellers (file 30, p. 4, file 36, p. 2, file 42, p. 13).
