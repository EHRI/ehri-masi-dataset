Annie Jacob: Personal account

1 folder

Annie Jacob was a cousin of the donor's mother's uncle and distantly related to Theo Marx. Before the war Annie lived in Nuremberg where she worked at the French embassy. She along with her mother left Germany for France where for a period they were interned at Gurs.
