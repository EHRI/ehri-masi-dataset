Oral history interview with Ivan Trofimov

1 videocassette (DVCAM), sound, color ; 1/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. The interview was directed and supervised by Nathan Beyrak. The interview was conducted with Ivan Trofimov in Latvia for the Latvia Documentation Project on August 27, 2005. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in March 2006.
