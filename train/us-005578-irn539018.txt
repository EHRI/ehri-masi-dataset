Oral history interviews with Ninette DePomme, Norbert Bikales, and André Lelong

1 videocasette (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Lisa Gossels and Dean Wetherell, producers and directors of the documentary "The Children of Chabannes" (1999), donated the oral history interviews with Ninette DePomme, Norbert Bikales, and André Lelong and related footage used in the making of the film to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in June 2014.
