Oral history interview with Georg Diers

5 videocassettes (MiniDV),

4 sound cassettes (90 min.),

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, in association with the Institut fuer Geschichte und Biographie der Fernuniversitaet Hagen, coordinated the interview with Georg Diers in Germany, on July 6, 2004. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in December 2004.
