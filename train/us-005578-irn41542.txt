Dachau: Two Perspectives

Presentation by Julius Eisenstein, George Katzman, and Neil S. Eichelbaum

2 DVD-ROMs, 4 3/4 in.

Neil S. Eichelbaum donated the recording, originally entitled "Dachau: Two Perspectives," to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in May 2010. The presentation was recorded at Miami Lakes Educational Center in May 2009.
