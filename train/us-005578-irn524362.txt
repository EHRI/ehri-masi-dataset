folder

1

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Collection consists of documents and photographs relating to the Kupperschlag and Isaac families. Ruth and her sister Marion left Germany on Kindertransport for Holland; their parents were taken to Theresienstadt, and wrote from there shortly before their deportation to Auschwitz in September 1944. Ruth and Marion survived Vught, Auschwitz, and several other camps; the parents were murdered in Auschwitz in September 1944.
