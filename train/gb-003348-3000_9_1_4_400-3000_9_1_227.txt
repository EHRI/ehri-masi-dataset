Correspondence with Buchrucker, Hasso

5 items

Hasso Buchrucker (born 1935) is a German jurist and diplomat. He is the son of Bruno Ernst Buchrucker, a right wing officer of the German Army, member of the NSDAP, and supporter of Otto Strasser, who later became in conflict with the Nazis to some extent.

Handwritten and typewritten letters concerning an enquiry for information material on Bruno Ernst Buchrucker. Contained is a memo outlining the view of historian Thilo Vogelsang on the person of interest.
