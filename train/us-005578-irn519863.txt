Der Stürmer, Nummer 16, April 1938, 16. Jahr 1938

The newspaper was donated to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2008 by Ben Binder.

Julius Streicher was the founder of "Der Stürmer" and Gauleiter of Franconia. He was sentenced to death at the International Military Tribunal in Nuremberg. [Encyclopedia of the Holocaust. Vol. 3-4. New York: MacMillan Publishing Company, 1995, pp. 1788.]

One issue of the antisemitic newspaper Der Stürmer, from April 1938 with the headline: Die habsburger und die Juden.
