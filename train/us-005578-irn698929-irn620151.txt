Emil Gorovets: Gebirtig poetry

Poetry of Mordecai Gebirtig set to music by Ukrainian musician Emil Gorovets. Gebirtig was a Yiddish poet and songwriter who died in the Krakow Ghetto in 1942. Gorovets sent this cassette to USHMM in 1993 for consideration to be included on the 1994 album, "Krakow Ghetto Notebook: Mordecai Gebirtig".
