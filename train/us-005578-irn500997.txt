"Sturm 38" of the Sturmabteilung (SA) of the Nationalsozialistische Deutsche Arbeiter-Partei (NSDAP) was founded in 1926 in Wiesbaden.

This logbook documents "Sturm 38," a Wiesbaden unit of the Sturmabteilung (SA), from 1926 to 1933. The book includes detailed entries and descriptions of educational, propaganda, and ideological activities, marches, trips, funerals, weapons training, and fights with communists.
