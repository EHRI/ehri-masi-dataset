Oral history interview with Franc Pantar

1 videocassette (DVCAM), sound, color ; 1/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. Nathan Beyrak, Project Director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Franc Pantar on November 21, 2009 as part of the Museum's Former Yugoslavia Witnesses Documentation Project in Slovenia.
