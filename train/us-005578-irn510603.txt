Oral history interview with Chaim Zelikovich Domnich

1 videocassette (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak, project director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Chaim Zelikovich Domnich on August 12, 1995. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in September 1995.
