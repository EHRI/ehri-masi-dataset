Oral history interview with Michael von Cranach

1 digital files, MPEG-4

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

This is a witness interview of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Perpetrators, Collaborators, and Witnesses: The Jeff and Toby Herr Testimony Initiative, a multi-year project to record the testimonies of non-Jewish witnesses to the Holocaust. Boris Neusius and Wendy Lower, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, conducted the interview with Dr. Michael von Cranach in Germany on December 2, 2010. The interview was received by the Museum's Oral History Branch in 2011.
