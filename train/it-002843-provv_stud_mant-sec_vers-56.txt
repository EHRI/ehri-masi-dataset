Busta n° 56

around 150 pages

In this folder there are several kind of documents, mainly: 
“personal files” made by teachers and employees of Mantua public schools declaring their racial belonging (Jewish or “Arian”); 
documents related to an “Exhibition of the Race”, organized all over Italy; 
orders for replacing geographical maps made by Jewish scholars with others made by “Arian” authors (May, 1939); 
census of Jewish teachers, students and special cases of racial determination (most of the time, siblings of mixed marriages).
