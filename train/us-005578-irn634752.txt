Yiṿo in Argenṭine

Hager was born in Romania in 1898 and died in Buenos Aires in 1985. He arrived in Argentina in1952 with a group of refugee artists. He was the mastermind behind the press project and a key person in the forging of the postwar organization of Jewish cultural life.

Articles and press clippings organized by Boruch Hager, a Jewish refugee in Argentina. Records relate to antisemitism, the Holocaust, and the arrival of Jewish refugees and survivors to Argentina. Included in the collection are indexes, clippings, photographs, and other materials.
