Oral history interview with Floris Kalman

1 digital file, MP4

Mike Zervos donated the interviews of the B’nai B’rith Courage to Care (Vic) collection to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in May 2020. The Courage to Care collection was produced as an initiative of B'nai B'rith to inform and educate Australians about the dangers of prejudice, racism, and discrimination.
