Collection includes “After Action Report” of the 914th artillery; photographs of Orban Wales Peters, Jr.'s unit on march into Germany and at Camp Phillip Morris; correspondence; miscellaneous Army passes; correspondence from Margarete Lang Schweidler, from Germany, to her daughter Gretel Schweidler Holzer in the U.S. Her daughter, Anna Margarete Holzer married Orban Wales Peters, Jr. They are the parents of the donor.
