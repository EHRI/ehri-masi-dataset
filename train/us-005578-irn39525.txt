folder

1

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Collection of photographs documenting the Buchenwald and Ohrdruf concentration camps immediately following liberation; images include burial of the victims, mass graves, and general scenes of the camps. Contains twenty photographs mounted with tape onto recto and verso of small black photo album pages, some with captions handwritten across top in blue ink; dated April 1945. Includes an image of Major Bernard Block, DDS (donor's father) in his military uniform; dated July 1945.
