United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

One bound copy of a manuscript related to the life of Marie Warschawsky (1881-1964). The manuscript contains handwritten testimony and copies of personal documents and photographs. Mrs. Warschawsky was a nurse in Russia before moving to France in 1922. When the war began, she worked for the Ministry for Labor under the German occupation and spent the war in Paris.
