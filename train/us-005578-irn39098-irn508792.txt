Oral history interview with Johannes Baptist Dominious Steinfort and Maria Steinfort-Deviojer

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, in cooperation with Watchtower Bible and Tract Society, Inc. produced the interview with Johannes Baptist Dominious Steinfort & Maria Steinfort-Deviojer on February 2, 1991.
