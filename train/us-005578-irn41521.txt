Oral history interview with Giacomo Nunez

1 digital file, WAV

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Peggy Frankston, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the interview with Giacomo Nunez on April 16, 2010 in Chevy Chase, MD. This interview is part of a series of USHMM-produced interviews about Jewish life in countries in North Africa during the Holocaust.
