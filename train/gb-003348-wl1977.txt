Müller family: Resitution claim

1 folder

A claim by the surviving next of kin of Leopold and Irene Müller of Bad Kissingen, Germany, who, it is claimed were forced to sell their business to a local Nazi party member. He was unsuccesfull in gaining restitution in a post war court case on the grounds of insufficient proof.

Copy court judgement with covering letter from Jewish Resitution Successor Organisation
