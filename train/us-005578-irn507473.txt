Oral history interview with Ernest Lorge

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois

The Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois (now Illinois Holocaust Museum and Education Center) conducted the interview with Ernest Lorge on April 27, 1984. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History branch received the tape of the interview from the Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois on December 12, 1989.
