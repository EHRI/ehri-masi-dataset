Oral history interview with Doris (Don) Duplechein

1 videocassette (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

Orange County California Anti-Defamation League

David Kennedy on behalf of the Anti-Defamation League, Orange County, California, conducted the interview with Doris (Don) Duplechein on May 21, 1993. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the copy of the interview from the Anti-Defamation League, Orange County, California on May 3, 2000.
