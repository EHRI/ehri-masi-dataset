Oral history interview with Sibylle Niemӧller

1 sound cassette (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Sibylle Niemӧller in Philadelphia, Pa., on November 21, 1986. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on October 17, 2000.
