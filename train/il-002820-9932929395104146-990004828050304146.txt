[Letter from Lieven concerning sent reports]

1 electronic resource (3 pages)

Letter to Todtli from rince Anatole Lieven in Latvia, concerning a report he sent to the Erfurt address. The report is incomplete due to lack of documentation, and pleads his health as a reason for his inability to come to Bern. He likewise says that he will send to Erfurt a note concerning the statement of intent made by the world Zionist Congress found on a Red Army comissar killed in 1919.
