Oral history interview with Suzi Weiss

8 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak conducted the interview with Suzi Weiss in Israel on June 9, 1995. The U.S.Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in 1996, as an accretion to the original collection of Israel Documentation Project interviews received by transfer in February1995.
