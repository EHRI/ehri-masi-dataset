Cukier and Cohen family letters

folder

1

Letters between the Cukier brothers (Szmulek and Jakub, in Palestine and in Poland) and their uncles, Max and Charles Cohen, in United States. The letters date from the1920s until approximately 1946. Includes a 1924 affidavit in which Max Cohen attests to his ability to provide for his nephew, Szmulek Cukier, should he be allowed to immigrate. Postwar letters from Jakub Cukier relate the fate of his parents and brother in Poland.
