Magdalena Kusserow (later Magdalena Reuter) was persecuted by the Nazis because of her faith as a Jehovah's Witness. She was imprisoned in the Bibelforscher barrack at Ravensbrück and was liberated in 1945 by the Soviet Army.

The letter was written by Magdalena Kusserow, a Jehovah's Witness, from Ravensbrück concentration camp to her sister, Annemarie Kusserow, in April 1942.
