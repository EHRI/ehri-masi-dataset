[Index of US forces regarding files of the Reichsrundfunkgesellschaft]

1 electronic resource (116 pages)

The file details efforts by the US occupation forces in Germany to compile an idex of files pertaining to the inner workings of the Reichsrundfunkgesellschaft, the official organ behind radio transmission services in Germany before and during World War II from 1926 until the end of World War II in 1945.
