Susan Weiss papers

folder

1

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Contains a birth certificate for Siegfried Schaffer (donor's father), born in Niebylec, Poland on 12th of April 1893; a certificate stating that Henia Booth (donor's mother) was born in Błażowa, Poland, in 1898; a matriculation diploma issued to Henia Booth in Rzeszów in September 1921; Ketubah of donor's parents dated July 3, 1927; correspondence; a family book in which the names and birthdates of all the children are registered; a Kinderausweis issued to Susi Schaffer on February 20, 1939, and other documents concerning the Schaffer family.
