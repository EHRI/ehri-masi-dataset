Das Schicksal der Kinder von Lidice

1 electronic resource (3 Pages)

The report by Karl Raddatz describes the incidents that happened around the 10. June in 1942 in the Czech village Lidice where SS killing squads murdered all male inhabitants of the village and deported the women and children on Hitler's order to revenge the death of Heydrich. Some of the children were examined and taken into Lebensborn homes were they were Germanized and the rest was sent to the Lodz children Camp and probably murdered there.
