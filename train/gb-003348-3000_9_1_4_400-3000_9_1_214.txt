Correspondence with Brockway, Fenner

2 letters

Archibald Fenner Brockway, Baron Brockway (1888-1988) was a British journalist and politician. The pacifist and nuclear opponent was a long-term MP before he was made Life Peer and joined the House of Lords in 1964.

C. C. Aronsfeld informs A. F. Brockway of an invitation to a private meeting of Mps he had received. At this meeting he is supposed to present The Wiener Library arcival material relevant for a planned bill against incitement to racial hatred.
