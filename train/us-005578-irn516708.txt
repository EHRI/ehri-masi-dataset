Krieger family papers

folders

The Krieger family papers consist of correspondence and postcards written by Stefania Krieger and her son, Henryk [donor's half-brother], in Tarnopol, Poland (now Ternopil, Ukraine), and Tarnów, Poland, to Stefania's husband, Stanislaw [donor's father], in Buzău, Baile Govora, and Slatina, Romania.
