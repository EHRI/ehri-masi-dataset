Oral history interview with Vilem Kriz

2 videocassettes (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Vilem Kriz on May 20, 1992 and February 16, 1993. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in December 1999.
