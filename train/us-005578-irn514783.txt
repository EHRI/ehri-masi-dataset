Alfred Eisner (1932-2015) was born on 5 April 1932 in Teplice-Å anov, Czechoslovakia (Teplice, Czech Republic) to Joseph and Maria Eisner. He was deported to Theresienstadt in 1944. After liberation he was united with his family and later immigrated to the United States.

Consists of six photographs of the Eisner family of Prague, Czechoslovakia. Includes photographs of Rudolph and Karel Eisner, and Elsa, Laura, and Klara Eisnerova. All pictured perished in Auschwitz.
