Eva Ehrlich papers

Testimony: Written in form of novel, bound typescript, three volumes, titled "Episode, novel by Francis Hevesi," copyrighted by Eva Ehrlich in 1981. Also contains documents including letter written to Hevesi in 1942, appealing for assistance in finding missing family members, and a letter from Hevesi, in English, written after his immigration to U.S., addressed to Bill Stern, describing events in Hungary in 1944.
