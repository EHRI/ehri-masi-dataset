Correspondence and related records regarding the history of restitution (Wiedergutmachung)

box

1

Includes correspondence, notes, critical reviews, and draft letters related to Dr. Walter Schwarz's "Rückerstattung Nach den Gestzen der Allüerten Mächte," a history of restitution. Also included are correspondence and notes related to the publication of a pamphlet on the history of the United Restitution Organization by Norman Bentwich.
