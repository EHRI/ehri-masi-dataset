Gebietskommissariat of Kamianets-Podilskyi

33 files

Collection contains regulations of wages, correspondence with the Gebietskommissar, city administration and other institutions on various issues, acts of the description of the property of the Jews and its sale, accounting documents, minutes of the meeting of the Ukrainian committee, accounting books.

Collection includes document containing information about liquidation of ghettos in various localities of Kamianets-Podilskyi gebiet (file 1).
