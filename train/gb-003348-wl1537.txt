Stockholm International Forum on the Holocaust

1 folder

The Stockholm International Forum on the Holocaust brought together presidents, prime ministers and other high-ranking officials from 40 countries to discuss how to keep Holocaust remembrance alive; the lessons to be drawn; and how best to pass on the knowledge of the fact to future generations.

This collection contains transcripts of speeches by a number of leading European political figures and Professor Yehuda Bauer of Yad Vashem, Jerusalem, on the occasion of the Stockholm International Forum on the Holocaust.
