Marianne Fischer: copy correspondence

1 folder

Many of the relatives of Marianne Fischer were murdered in concentration camps during the Holocaust. She and her husband Hans Fischer seem to have emigrated to England via Italy.

This collection contains copy correspondence from Rosa Jonas in Berlin to her granddaughter Marianne Frank who had recently emigrated from Nazi Germany with her husband Hans Fischer.<br /><div>Rosa Jonas writes about news from family and friends in Berlin, some of whom were also emigrating. Includes summaries of letters in English.</div>
