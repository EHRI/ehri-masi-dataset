Victor Zorza: Press cuttings and news copy

2 boxes

These press cuttings and newspaper copy relating to anti-Semitism in the Soviet Union were apparently gathered and/ or produced by Victor Zorza, the famous Kremlinologist.<br />

Victor Zorza, a Jew, born at Lvov, Poland, in 1939, was the Guardian Newspaper's Kremlin expert for 25 years, renowned for first detecting the ideological split between Moscow and Peking and for predicting the Russian invasion of Czechoslovakia in 1968.<br />
