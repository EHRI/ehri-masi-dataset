Klappholttal Youth Camp

1 folder

Born out of the ethos of the prevailing youth movements, the Klappholz Youth Camp camp was founded in 1919 to provide for the spiritual and intellectual as well as the physical needs of young people. It still exists today.

This collection of published and unpublished pamphlets and prospectuses provides a history of the Klappholttal Youth Camp, Schleswig Holstein.
