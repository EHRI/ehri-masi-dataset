The <em>Reichsvertretung der Deutschen Juden</em>, began as the <em>Reichsvertretung der Juden in Deutschland</em> in 1935, and became the <em>Reichsvereinigung der Juden in Deutschland in</em> 1939. It came into being shortly after the Nazi seizure of power as the first body representative of all German Jewry, the main objective of which, was to deal with the serious problems facing Germany Jewry from the new, antisemitic regime.
