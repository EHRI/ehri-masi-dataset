Nationale Mahn- und Gedenkstäte Ravensbrück and Sachsenhausen

Includes copies of documents concerning the administration of Ravensbrück concentration camp for women. The documents relate to the distribution of labor in the camp, names of kommandos and overseers, and transports. Also included is a separate file for Harry Hüttel, a political prisoner at Sachsenhausen.
