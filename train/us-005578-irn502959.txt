Oral history interview with Dov Nir

8 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak conducted the interview with Dov Nir in Israel on March 28, 1994. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview on March 1, 1995, as an accretion to the original collection of Israel Documentation Project interviews received by transfer in February 1995.
