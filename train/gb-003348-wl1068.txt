Dr Salo Windschauer: copy examination certificate

1 folder

Dr Salo Windschauer, the subject of the document, was born 29 October 1913, deported to Terezin with the 13th transport (10 October 1942), and deported to Auschwitz 28 September 1944

<p>Copy doctor's examination certificate of Dr Salo Windschauer</p><p>When this copy document was deposited it formed the frontispiece to a photocopy extract from Totenbuch Theresienstadt I Deportierte aus Oesterreich, the original of which the Wiener Library already holds.</p>
