Abend and Getter family collection

folders

7

Photographs and letter pertaining to Muni and Chaya (Silberman) Getter, and Salomon and Paulette (Rosiner) Abend. The Getters and Abends were originally from Przemysl, Poland, but moved to France in the interwar period. Muni and Paulette survived the Holocaust and married after the deaths of their spouses during the Holocaust.
