Esther Gitman papers

box

1

Contains correspondence and related documents concerning the wartime experiences of Zlatko Najman (or Neumann), of Zagreb, Croatia. Includes extensive correspondence from Najman to his fiancée, Olga Rajsek, from 1941-1944, including the period when Najman was interned at a prisoner-of-war camp in Osnabrück, Germany; copy print photographs of Najman and others; a pocket calendar used by Najman in 1943; and a pension identification booklet from the Yugoslavian army, assigned to Najman, circa 1947-1960.
