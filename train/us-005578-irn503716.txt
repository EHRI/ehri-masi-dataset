Elsner, Kurt M.

Hertha Mirabeau was born Hertha Königsberger on 21 August 1879 in Krefeld, Germany to Leopold and Jenny (née Wilmerdörfer) Königsberger. She was deported to Theresienstadt in July 1942. On 23 October 1944 she was sent on Transport 467 to Auschwitz where she perished.

The collection consists of a letter written by Hertha Mirabeau (Königsberger) in Krefeld, Germany in April 1942 to her daughter. Hertha was deported to Theresienstadt several months later.
