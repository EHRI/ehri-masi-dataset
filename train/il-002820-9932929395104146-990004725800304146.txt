[Letter by Thomas Philipp, Berlin Wannsee, to Mr. Zeman, Wiener Library London, concerning files on the topic of Nazi Propaganda held by the Berlin Documents Centre]

1 electronic resource (3 pages)

This file contains a letter by Thomas Philipp from Berlin Wannsee to Mr. Zeman of the Wiener Library London dated 19.09.1962. The letter is a first short report about the situation at the Berlin Documents Centre concerning files on the topic of Nazi Propaganda. Philipp promises in his letter to send a more detailed report and the index of the files located in Berlin the other week, as there is still a lot of material by the Reichsrundfunkgesellschaft (Reich Broadcasting Company), that he did not look through yet. Also included is the mentioned index of the files located in Berlin and comments on the quality and usefulness of the material.
