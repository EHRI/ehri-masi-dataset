Ferenc Fishlovics collection

folder

1

Most of the documents in the collection relate to the death of Ferenc Fishlovics (donor's father). Collection contains a postcard from Fishlovic, written from a labor camp in 1942; an affidavit from 1953 relating to his fate; a death notice from 1954; a portion of an undated document relating to Fishlovics' death and disappearance; and a photocopy of an undated photograph of Fischlovics; the original was transfered to the Photo Archives.
