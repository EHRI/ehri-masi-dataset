Oral history interview with Gary Rosenthal

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Esther Toporek Finder, President of Generations of the Shoah - Nevada, produced the oral history interview with Gary Rosenthal in partnership with Raymode Fiol, President of the Holocaust Survivors Group of Southern Nevada, Brett Levner, film professor at the University of Nevada Las Vegas (UNLV), and Sun City Anthem TV in Henderson, NV.
