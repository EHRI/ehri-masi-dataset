Sonja Haid Greene (Siauliai, Lithuania) was born to a Jewish family and after the war immigrated to the United States.

The collection consists of a musical score, in pencil, entitled "Fantasie in Gelb"; a song written in pencil “Ein Traum” in Dachau concentration camp; Camp # I (Landsberg/Lech) from Mr. Haid to donor, 1945; “Mamale” written in 1943 Ghetto Kaunas, Lithuania; photo of Percy Haid; and five letters from Percy Haid (donor's husband).
