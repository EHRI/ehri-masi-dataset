Der Stadthauptmann der Stadt Krakau

Starosta miasta Krakowa (Sygn. 228)

103 digital files, PDF

Żydowski Instytut Historyczny im. Emanuela Ringelbluma

Contains records from the Stadthauptmann in Kraków (Generalgouvernement), including questionnaires, identification and registration cards, lists of deported Jews, registry records of the Jewish community (birth certificates), documents relating to confiscation of Jewish property, documents belonging to Jewish firms from Kraków , leaflets, and other documents related to Jewish life in Kraków during the occupation.
