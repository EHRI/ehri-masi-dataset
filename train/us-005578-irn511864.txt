Oral history interview with Shalom Schreiber

5 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

The interview with Shalom Schreiber was conducted on October 25, 1987 by Eli Pfefferkorn for the Krakow Underground Project. The project was coordinated by the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Council in Israel with Holocaust survivors who were members of the Krakow Underground.
