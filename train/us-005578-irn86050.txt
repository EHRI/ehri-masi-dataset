Oral history interview with Agnes Broschinski, Gertrud Friedrich Braun, Gerhard Braun, and Konrad Stein

7 , MPEG-2

Fortunoff Video Archive for Holocaust Testimonies

Shloime Perel, on behalf of the Refugee Research Project associated with McGill University, donated copies of Gabrielle Tyrnauer's oral history interview with Agnes Broschinski, Gertrud Friedrich Braun, Gerhard Braun, and Konrad Stein to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2013. Gabrielle Tyrnauer produced the interview on behalf of the Fortunoff Video Archive for Holocaust Testimonies at Yale University.
