Oral history interview with Ben Zion Blushtein

6 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak conducted the interview with Blushtein ben Zion in Israel on July 3, 1991, for the Israel Documentation Project. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview by transfer from the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Dept. in February 1995.
