Photograph of four Gedolim

folder

1

Consists of one pre-war photograph taken in Békèscsaba, Hungary, of four gedolim, wise men and leaders of the Orthodox community. Elsa Schwartz [donor] identifies the men as (from left to right): Rebbe Ratz Feld, Rebbe Shulem Lezer, Tvi Elimelech-Gabay, and Rebbe Shulem Lezer Halberstam.
