Andrew Laszlo papers

folder

1

The papers consist of ten postcards and four letters written and recieved by Andras Laszlo [donor] in Kó́szeg, Hungary, and Zsibo, Romania, to and from Erzsebet Laszlo [donor's mother], Laszlo Laszlo [donor's father], Miklos Laszlo [donor's uncle], Alice Laszlo [donor's aunt], and Sandor Laszlo [donor's brother] in Szombathely, Hungary, as well as one poem written by Andras Laszlo in Zsibo, Romania.
