Roman Morrow photograph collection

folder

1

The collection consists of eight photographs documenting Roman Morrow and his family's experiences in Kraków, Poland, during the Holocaust. Includes images of Roman Morrow's Maccabi soccer team, members of a Zionist organization, his brother, Steven Morrow, in the Polish Army, Montelupich prison in Kraków, forced labor in the swamps of Pychowiec, a funeral procession, and Roman Morrow in the Kraków ghetto wearing a Star of David armband.
