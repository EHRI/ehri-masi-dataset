Gertrude Ehrlich, who fled Vienna and immigrated to the US in 1939 with her parents Josef and Charlotte (Kobak) Ehrlich and sister. The collection includes: · Prewar family photographs · Marriage Certificate of Josef and Charlotte Ehrlich, July 31, 1939 · Charlotte (Kobak) Ehrlich's US Certificate of Naturalization January 24, 1945 · Margarete Ehrlich’s US Certificate of Naturalization, January 24, 1945 · Margarete Ehrlich’s Meldungsbuch, Universität zu Wien (notification ID book)
