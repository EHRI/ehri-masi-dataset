Selected records of the Ministry of Finance, Bulgaria

10 microfilm reels, 35 mm

T︠S︡entralen dŭrzhaven arkhiv (Bulgaria)

Contains correspondence and other documents relating to expenses and consequences of the establishment of anti-Jewish laws and regarding ownership of real estate and houses of Jewish origin. Incudes applications for payment of compensation for the currency confiscated by the banks, 1920-1921, letters of expropriation of shares of persons of Jewish origin 1941-1943, minutes of the Council of Ministers with attached files on the basis of Article 9 of the Law Against Speculation with real estate, owned by persons of Jewish origin, 1942, 1943, orders of the Council of Ministers, and registers of names.
