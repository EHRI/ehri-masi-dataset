Oral history interview with Hanna Zwang

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois

The Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois (now Illinois Holocaust Museum and Education Center) conducted the interview with Hanna Zwang on December 11, 1986. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History branch received the tape of the interview from the Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois on December 12, 1991.
