Oral history interview with Anatole Zylberstein

1 videocasette (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Lisa Gossels and Dean Wetherell, producers and directors of the documentary "The Children of Chabannes" (1999), donated the oral history interview with Anatole Zylberstein and related footage used in the making of the film to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in June 2014.
