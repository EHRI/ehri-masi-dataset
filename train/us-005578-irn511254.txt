Nanette Jakobs-DeVries was born Nanette Martha de Vries on 3 June 1938 in Amersfoort, Netherlands to BM de Vries-Kolthoff and Harry Karol de Vries. She had one brother, Harry David de Vries (1941-1943). The siblings were hidden under the names of Netje Schutte and Hansje Seubering.
