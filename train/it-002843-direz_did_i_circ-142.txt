Registro 142 M (1939-40)

Approximately 200 pages

School register of male students of public Mantua schools. It includes also a school register of the Mantua elementary school for students “of Jewish race”, run by Renato Rovighi, with all the names, date and place of birth, names of the parents, and school marks, of male and female students: Arianna Cremonini, Franco Cremonini, Giuliano Cremonini, Fernanda Levi, Rita Levi, Vittorio Castelletti, Davide Cremonini, Lidia Gallico, Emma Parigi, Giuditta Levi, Norma Cremonini, Luisa Levi, Giannino Revere.
