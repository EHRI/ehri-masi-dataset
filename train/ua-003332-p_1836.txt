Bureau of the House management No. 7 of the Sumy city administration

55 files

Collection contains house-register books, lists of workers in house management and tenants. Inventory information, orders for apartments. Information on the payment of wages.

Collection includes house-registers of Sumy dwellers during the German occupation, including the Jewish dwellers (file 2, p. 5, file 5, pp. 3, 4, file 45, pp. 15, 16).
