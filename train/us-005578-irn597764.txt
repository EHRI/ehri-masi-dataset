Ministry of Foreign Affairs : Martial law, arrests and internment (Group 84.A.21a-b)

3,697 digital images, JPEG

Statens arkiver (Denmark)

Records relating to martial law in Denmark during the WWII, including: announcements, reports from police districts and local authorities, as well as records relating to arrests of hostages, including lists of arrested Danes and releases, and seizures of buildings and property. Note: Records are restricted, access only by the permission of the Danish National Archives. Access is granted by application and must go through the Danish Data Protection Agency since the material contains personal information.
