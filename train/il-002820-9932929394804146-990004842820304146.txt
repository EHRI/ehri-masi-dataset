[letter from Brunschvig to Saly Mayer]

1 electronic ressource (2 pages)

This file contains a letter written by Brunschvig in 1936 to the President of the SIG (Swiss israelite Community) Saly Mayer. Mayer asked him what the outcome of the Bern trials should mean to the public abroad. Brunschvig answers in this letter with several convincing arguments, hoping to put an end to the rising antisemitism worldwide.
