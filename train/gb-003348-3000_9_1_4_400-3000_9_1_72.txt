Correspondence with Anders, Günther

3 letters

Dr. Günther Anders (born Günther Siegmund Stern, 1902 - 1992) was an Austrian philosopher and author of Jewish-German descent. From 1929-33 he was married to Hannah Arendt.

Correspondence regarding an enquiry on political and occupational activities of Bavarian minister of cultural affairs Theodor Maunz in the Nazi period.
