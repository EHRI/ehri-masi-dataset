Oral history interview with Anna Sonnenschein, Sheina Sachar-Gertner, and several unidentified individuals

1 sound cassette (90 min.),

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Anna Sonnenschein, Sheina Sachar-Gertner, and several unidentified individuals on April 14, 1983. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in July 2007.
