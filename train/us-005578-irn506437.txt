Jewish council in Krakau

Rada Żydowska miasta Krakowa (Sygn. 218)

1,211 digital images, JPEG

1 microfilm reel (digitized), 35 mm

Żydowski Instytut Historyczny im. Emanuela Ringelbluma

Contains lists, correspondence, reports, instructions, and various other documents relating to the activities of the Judenrat (Jewish council) in Kraków, Poland, during the German occupation; evacuation and transports of Jewish citizens from Kraków; Jews in forced labor; the murder of Jewish physicians from Kraków and Kielce; aid for the poor Jews of Kraków; activities of the Jewish police in Kraków; Jewish welfare centers in Kraków; and issuance of identification cards to Jews in Kraków. Also contains a list of names of Kraków Judenrat members.
