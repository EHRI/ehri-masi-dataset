Walter Furst papers

folders

2

Correspondence between Walter Furst (donor's father) at Camp Ritchie, Maryland and his brother Harry, his parents, T.E. Glaser, Herbert Meyer, Curt Sloan, Fred Furst, R.H. Stern, Werner Oppenheim, and various other friends scattered across the United States. Content of the letters focus mainly on military life and other personal matters. Responses from Walter are all typed or carbon copies of sent correspondence. The majority of letters are in English, though some from Walter's father are in German. Bulk dated September-December 1943 (some dated 1942); also included are two overdue notices for a library book.
