Oral history interview with Lili Brody-Carmosino

1 digital file, WAV

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Ina Navazelskis, Program Coordinator for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the interview with Lili Brody-Carmosino on October 16, 2009. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in January 2010.
