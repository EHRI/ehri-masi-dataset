Israelitische Kultusgemeinde, Nuremberg: Letter re Spear family

1 folder

Nothing is known about the provenance of this copy letter.

It is almost certain that this is the same family, which manufactured Spear's games. Note that the <i>Gedenkbuch für die Nürnberger Opfer der Schoa</i> list members of the Spear family as having been transported to Izbica on 24 March 1942.
