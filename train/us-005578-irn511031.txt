Oral history interview with Avraham Tory

2 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Sandra Bradley, a film production consultant for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, conducted the interview with Avraham Tory on July 23, 1997 in preparation for the exhibition "Hidden History of the Kovno Ghetto," which opened in November 1997. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received a copy of the interview on July 11, 1998.
