Wiener Library Collection of Testimonies, London, 1940-1957

1171 files

After World War II, the Wiener Library in London continued to gather documentation regarding the Holocaust in Europe, including testimonies of Holocaust survivors. Most of the testimonies were gathered from 1954 to 1960. In 1980 a large part of the archives was transferred to Tel Aviv University.Copies of the collection of testimonies were submitted to Yad Vashem in the early 1980s.
