Joseph L. Pavia (1911-1994) was born September 10, 1911. He married Dorothea Jeanne Hornbake (1918-2013), and the couple had three children: Cathy, Hal, and Daniel. He served with the 12th Armored Division during World War II.

Photographs of the Landsberg concentration camp immediately after liberation, from the donor's father, Lt. Joseph Pavia, who served in the12th Armored Division.
