Ruth Dina Curtis: personal papers

1 folder

Ruth Dina Curtis (nee Heideman) was born into an orthodox Jewish family in 1920, the daughter of the senior cantor at Halberstadt synagogue, North Germany. She came to Great Britain via Hamburg at age 18.

This collection contains copy correspondence of Ruth Dina Curtis' family members from the 1940s, an identity card for Herbert Cohn and a biographical account in English of Ruth Dina Curtis (nee Heideman) by her son.
