Saugumo policijos ir SD vado Lietuvai Lietuviškasis Kauno skyrius

One subfonds and 547 files. Some documents are handwritten.

Various documents on the activities of the staff of Lithuanian SS and SD department (includes documents about collaboration with local partisans – for example: reports by the leader of the Vilijampolė partisan unit to the chief of the security department of the Kaunas administrative region about arrested Jews on June 23, 1941; list of residents (mostly Jews) arrested by partisans of Vilijampolė partisan unit), orders to the Auxiliary police, applications from residents to release relatives who were in custody. Documents and correspondence with the Lithuanians state security about attitude of the locals toward Jews, etc. Reports about arrested Jews from all of Kaunas County.
