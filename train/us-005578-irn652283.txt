Oral history interview with Sonia Manes, Zena (Manes) Dickstein, and Rosalyn Friedman

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Ina Navazelskis, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the oral history interview with Sonia Manes, Zena (Manes) Dickstein, and Rosalyn Friedman on January 22, 2019 in Aventura, FL.
