Shlomo Baruch Blumberg's his parents Moshe Blumberg and Sheindl Rachel Steinlauf and sister Sara Zosia Kliger were murdered in the Warsaw ghetto; Frima Blumberg Maiman, his other sister, survived in hiding.

Contains a photo album with images of activities and vacations with friends of Shlomo Baruch Blumberg (donor's father) who immigrated to Palestine in 1934 from Warsaw, Poland.
