Oral history interview with Dov Levin

5 videocassettes (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Randy M. Goldman, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the oral history interview with Dov Levin on November 10, 1994. This interview is one of 51 oral histories included in the Miles Lerman Center for the Study of Jewish Resistance.
