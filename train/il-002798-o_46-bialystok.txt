Hakibbutz Ha'arzi Archives Collection: The Bialystok Ghetto Uprising

1 microfilm reel

Most of the documentation in this Collection comes from Haika Grossman and includes her testimonies regarding the Uprising in Bialystok, a report concerning the activities of the fighters from the Hechalutz Movement, which was submitted to the main headquarters of the Partisan Movement in 1944 and more.
