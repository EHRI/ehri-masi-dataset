Oral history interview with Chaim Chacham

2 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak, project director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Chaim Chacham on November 18, 1999. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview on August 31, 2001.
