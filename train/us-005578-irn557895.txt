William Harris Pysher (1915-2005) was born January 28, 1915 in Pennsylvania to Floyd and Lottie Pysher. He married Helen Mae Saltern in 1937. During World War II he served with the 628th Medical Clearing Company, crossing the Rhine River and serving in the Battle of the Bulge.

Photographs taken by American serviceman, William Harris Pysher, shortly after the liberation of Buchenwald.
