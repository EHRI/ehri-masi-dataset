[Newspaper clippings from The Observer -1946, Russian control in Europe, and Press Conference Invitations]

1 electronic resource (29 pages)

Clippings from The Observer, many of them written by Paul Anderson, some on French politics, and De Gaulle'sreturn topower. Speculations and opinion pieces are offeredon the French political situation, the poorpositionFrenchsocialists find themselves in,and De Gaulle's future prospects. Many other articles concern the situation in Occupied Germany, especiallyin the Soviet-controlledzone, and discuss the Berlin referendum. Onearticcle is entitled Russian Antisemitism. Another article provides an extensive profile of Maurice Thorez, politician and leader of the French Communist Party. Additionally several typed invitations to press conferences are also attached.
