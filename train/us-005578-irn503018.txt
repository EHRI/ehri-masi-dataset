Oral history interview with Libi Epshtein

6 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak conducted the interview with Libi Epshtein in Israel on March 30, 1995. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in 1996, as an accretion to the original collection of Israel Documentation Project interviews received by transfer in February 1995.
