Charlotte Ader papers

folder

1

The papers consist of a birth certificate for administrative use issued by the Ministère des Affaires Étrangères in France for Abraham Blatt donor's father who was unable to provide proof of his original nationality and an identification card issued by the Fédération Nationale des Centres d'Entr'Aide des Internés et Deportés Politiques in France to Abraham Blatt concerning his internment in Auschwitz.
