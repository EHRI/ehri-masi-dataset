Yiddish Folk Songs -- Daniel Kempin

Yiddish song program by Daniel Kempin (singer & guitarist) in the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Meyerhoff Auditorium (one of the first recitals at USHMM). Side A: ver es hot in blat gelezn - aroys iz in Vilne (Vilna Ghetto) - papirosh - hungerik dayn ketsele (Krakow Ghetto) - s'brent! (Krakow Ghetto) - tsen brider (KZ Sachsenhausen) - mir lebn eybik! (Vilna Ghetto) - ven zingt a yid? - hulyet, hulyet, kinderlekh
