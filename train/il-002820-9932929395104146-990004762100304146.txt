Treatment of refugees in the United Kingdom

1 electronic resource (23 pages)

The file contains several informations from different organizations regarding the topic of the treatment of refugees in the United Kingdom. British organization collaborate closly with each other and represent all of their practical experience in dealing with refugees. A coordinating Committee for refugees in the United Kigdom has also been formed. Besides that there is a close cooperation between British and other organizations. The information given by other organizations are all regarding the refugee question. For example how they can organize themselves better, how they can help and reach even more refugees, how they can handle so many people from different countries and how they might simplify the daily life of the refugees in the United Kingdom.
