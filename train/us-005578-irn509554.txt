Oral history interview with Lenci Farkas

2 videocassettes (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Lenci Farkas on November 6, 1990 and December 11, 1990. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in December 2001.
