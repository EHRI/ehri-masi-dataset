Oral history interview with Jacob Barosin

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Harriet and Kenneth Kupferberg Holocaust Center at Queensborough Community College, CUNY

The interview with Jacob Barosin was conducted on June 28, 1989 by the Queensborough Community College Holocaust Resource Center and Archives. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received a copy of the interview in November 1990 and an interviewee release form on April 2, 1991.
