"Andre Spire, French Jewish poet and Zionist Activist"

Oral testimony of Marie-Brunette Spire

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Isabelle Pleskoff, head librarian at the Musée d'art et d'histoire du judaïsme (Mahj), donated video recordings of Holocaust presentations and interviews produced by the Mahj to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 2015. The interview was conducted on March 8, 2015 and the original French title is "André Spire poète français poète juif et militant sioniste 1868 1966 avec Marie-Brunette Spire."
