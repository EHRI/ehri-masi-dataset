The Federation of the Jewish Communities in the German Democratic Republic is sending its greetings and best wishes for success to the Conference of the World Jewish Congress in London on December 1st and 2nd. Jews in Germany were an important group of the victims in the Holocaust. The survivors who are living in the German Democratic Republic are feeling free in living their living and religion. They are offering their support to the World Jewish Congress. __ Agenda/schedule for the European Conference on Anti-Semitism December 1st -2nd 1962.
