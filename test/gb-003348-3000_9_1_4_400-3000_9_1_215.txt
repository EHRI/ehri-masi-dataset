Correspondence with Brodetsky, S.

9 letters

Prof. Dr. Selig Brodetsky (1888–1954) was a Russian born English mathematician and president of the Hebrew University in Jerusalem. He also joined the board of The Wiener Library in 1946.

Correspondence regarding Brodetsky’s cancellation of his Wiener Library Bulletin subscription, and a request for renewed financial support by the Jewish Agency for Palestine.
