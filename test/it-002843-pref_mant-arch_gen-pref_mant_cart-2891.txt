Dissequestro beni ebraici

Around 400 pages

“Decreti di revoca di confisca dei beni ebraici”, that is decrees of un-seizure of Jewish belongings, made by a specific bureau (Ufficio stralcio amministrazione beni israeliti) of the Agricultural Bank of Mantua, in order to give them back to the legitimate owners. There are also copies of the original decrees of seizure of Jewish belongings.
