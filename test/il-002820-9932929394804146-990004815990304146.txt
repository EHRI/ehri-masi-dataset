[Übersetzung eines Artikels aus der "Fahne" (Snamja) v. 1903]

3 electronic ressources (6 pages)

The file contains two cover pages and an extract of a translated article from the Russian newspaper "Znamia". The cover pages are from the Archive für Zeitgeschichte Zürich and are entitled as "Beweise aus dem Russischen, 1903". The author of the article and the newspaper itself support the Protocols and insist on their authenticity. The article also includes a copy of the Protocols for the reader in order to convince him of their originality.
