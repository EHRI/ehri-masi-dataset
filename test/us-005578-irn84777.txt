אהרון כהן – ארכיון אישי

282 digital images, JPEG

Shomer ha-tsaʻir (Organization : Israel). Merkaz tiʻud ṿa-ḥeḳer

Aharon Cohen (1910-1980), born in Bessarabia, settled in Palestine in 1937; was a director of the Arab Department of the political party Mapam and a member of the Mapam Political Committee, 1948-1949.

Personal archive of Aharon Cohen (1910-1980) contains reports and correspondence with representatives of Ha Shomer Hatzair Movement in Romania in 1937-1940, also includes the personal notebook related to his mission in Romania in 1947-1948.
