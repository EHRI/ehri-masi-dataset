Oral history interview with Hans Mommsen

3 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

The interview with Hans Mommsen was conducted by Joan Ringelheim, Director of Oral History, on April 4, 2000, in Arlington, Va. The tapes of the interview were received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives on April 6, 2000.
