Oral history interviews with André Lelong and Robert Charbonnier

1 videocasette (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Lisa Gossels and Dean Wetherell, producers and directors of the documentary "The Children of Chabannes" (1999), donated the oral history interviews with André Lelong and Robert Charbonnier and related footage used in the making of the film to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in June 2014.
