Selected records from the Panevėžys Branch County Archives in Lithuania related to restitution of Jewish property

16,390 digital images, JPEG

Panevėžio apskrities archyvas

Records of various county agencies (State Notary, County Executive Council, People's Court, etc.) related to the restitution and inheritance of the individual and communal Jewish property located in the Panevėžys County, Lithuania after World War II.
