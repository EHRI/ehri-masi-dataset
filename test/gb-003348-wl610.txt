David Spector: Miscellaneous papers

12 folders

David Spector, a British born Jew, dedicated much of his life to fighting fascism in this country, having experienced at first hand, the effects of Nazi persecution on Holocaust survivors.

<p><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>Readers need to reserve a reading room terminal to access a digital version of this archive.</strong></span></p><p>This miscellaneous collection of mostly copy papers and news cuttings was deposited by David Spector in a number of separate deposits, the details of some of which have been recorded. They consist mainly of papers relating to the activities of British Fascists before during and after WWI</p>
