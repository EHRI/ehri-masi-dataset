Hakibbutz Ha'arzi Archives Collection: The Hashomer Hatzair Movement in Hungary and Slovakia

7 files on 1 microfilm reel

n the Record Group there are seven files containing Hashomer Hatzair documentation from Hungary and Slovakia.

In the Collection there is a journal kept by the "Hahagshama" group in Budapest, conducted from 1939 until the murder of the Jews of Hungary, including a description of the events and underground activities.  The journal was transferred to Eretz Israel by Zvi Erez, one of the survivors of the group.

In the Collection there is also documentation regarding the activities of the parachutists [from Eretz Israel], such as photograph albums and documentation of the activities of the parachutists Egon Roth [?] and Haviva Reich.
