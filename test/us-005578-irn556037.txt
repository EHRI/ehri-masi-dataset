Ernst Graf (1905-1966) was born in Berlin on November 12, 1905 to Joachim and Maria Graf. He married Dorothea Reichenthal in 1933. In summer 1938 they immigrated to the United States via the Netherlands. He became an American citizen and trained at Camp Ritchie. He served overseas in the US Army in France, Belgium, the Netherlands, and Germany and interrogated enemy prisoners.

Collection of documents, certificates, and passports relating to the experiences of Ernst Graf who fled Nazi occupation and then served in the US Army during WWII as one of the Ritchie Boys.
