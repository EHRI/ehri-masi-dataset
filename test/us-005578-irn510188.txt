Bluma Fischer papers

folder

1

Consists of three photographs of Bluma Fischer's family and friends in Zamość, Poland , before World War II; four photographs of Bluma Fischer and her friends in Chelyabinsk, USSR between 1943 and 1944; and twelve photographs of Bluma Fischer and her husband, Lajb Fischer, in the Wetzlar displaced persons camp in Germany between 1946 and 1947.
