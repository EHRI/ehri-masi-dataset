Pál Weinstein (1906-1979) was born on September 6, 1906, in S'́toraljaújhely, Hungary. He was a professor of ophthalmology and staff member of the Budapest Jewish Hospital.

Papers concerning the Holocaust experiences of Pál Weinstein of Sátoraljaújhely, Hungary. Included is his memoir, interviews with Weinstein, photographs, correspondence, articles, and documents regarding his medical career.
