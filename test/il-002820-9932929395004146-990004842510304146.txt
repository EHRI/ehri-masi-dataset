[Letter from Lifschitz to Saly Mayer]

1 electronic ressource (2 pages)

This file contains a letter written by the lawyer Lifschitz in 1936 to Saly Mayer. Both important figures in the Bern trial. Lifschitz tells Mayer in this letter about his accumulated proofs for Fleischhauer's activities with the National Socialists, not only in Germany but also in Switzerland.
