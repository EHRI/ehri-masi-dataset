Oral history interview with Max Haber

3 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Linda Kuzmack, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, conducted the interview with Max Haber on August 18, 1990 in Washington, DC. Tapes of the interview were transferred to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in February 1995.
