Oral history interview with Patrick Costrine, Samuel Durfey, Stephen Hord, and Joseph Dietrich

1 videocassette (VHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Holocaust Resource Center of Buffalo

Toby Ticktin Back of the Holocaust Resource Center of Buffalo conducted the interview with Patrick Costrine, Samuel Durfey, Stephen Hord, and Joseph Dietrich in August 1990 with the cooperation and support of WIVB-TV in Buffalo, N.Y. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History branch received copies of interviews from the Holocaust Resource Center of Buffalo from 1990 - 1993. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the collection by transfer from the Oral History branch in February 1995.
