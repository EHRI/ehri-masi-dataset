United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

The Henry Sharp collection consists of photographs, identification documents, and correspondence related to the immediate post-war experiences of Henry Sharp (born Henryk Ostrowski), originally of Krakow, Poland. The collection includes post-liberation concentration camp photographs, post-war identification documents attesting to his imprisonment at Buchenwald concentration camp, residence permits, and a document relating to a restitution claim.
