George Fogelson papers

folder

1

Consists of one photograph of Hilda Anker, the donor's mother, playing a concertina in Van Nuys, CA, in 1944. The concertina is part of a previous donation to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum. Also included is one bound copy of "Heimat-log auf hoher see" by Gustav Schroeder, captain of the MS Saint Louis. The book was published in 1949, and this copy is signed by the author.
