Dr. Max Brenner collection

folder

1

Collection consists of four family photographs and part of an identification card with a photograph of two children and an adult. Collection of pre-war photographs of the Eichenbrenner family in Poland and one identity document issued to Kraindl Eichenbrenner and her children Mordechai [donor] and Helen while they were living as displaced persons in Italy after their liberation. Donor, his parents and sisters survived the Demblin concentration camp and were liberated in Czestochowa. They lived in DP camps in Austria and Italy. Other members of their extended family were killed during the Holocaust.
