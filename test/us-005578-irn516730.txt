Robert White was a well-known collector, principally of John F. Kennedy memorabilia, on which he was a recognized expert, consulted by the Smithsonian and other institutions. White amassed the largest collection of Kennedy material in private hands, second only to that of the JFK Presidential Library.

The papers contain documents and copy prints of photographs relating to the Holocaust. These items were acquired by Robert L. White over an extended period of time.
