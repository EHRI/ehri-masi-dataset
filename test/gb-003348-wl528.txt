G.F.J. Bergmann: Material re Jewish alpinists in Germany

100 frames

<p><span style="color: #ff0000;"><strong>Readers need to reserve a terminal in the reading room to access a digital version of this archive.</strong></span></p><p>This microfilm collection documents an interest in mountain climbing in the 1920s and 1930s, in particular Jewish involvement in the sport. It includes a number of issues of alpinist journals with articles on the involvement of Jews in the sport by Bergmann. Also original correspondence on the subject.</p>
