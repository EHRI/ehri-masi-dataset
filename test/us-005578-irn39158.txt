Deutscher Weckruf und Beobachter and the Free American, November 30, 1939

The newspaper was donated to to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2006 by Philip Sherman.

Newspaper entitled Deutscher Weckruf und Beobachter and the Free American. dated November 30, 1939. The newspaper promoted German identity in the United States and sought to down play Nazism; in English; printed in the United States.
