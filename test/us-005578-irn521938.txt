"Account of the Anschluss and of Kristallnacht"

folder

1

Consists of an account, 5 pages, of the Anschluss and of Kristallnacht written in a composition booklet by Nelly Szabo Ullman in 1939. Nelly emigrated to the United States from Vienna in May 1939 at the age of 14. Also includes a typed English translation of the account.
