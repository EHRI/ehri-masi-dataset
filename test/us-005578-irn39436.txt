Oral history interview with Paula Goldlust Blue

3 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, in association with the Musée Mémorial du Camp de Rivesaltes, conducted the interview with Paula Goldlust Blue on August 7, 2009. The interview was received by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives Branch in January 2009.
