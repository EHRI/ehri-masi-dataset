William Cygelfarb photographs

folder

1

Contains 45 post-war photographs of William Cygelfarb and many of the children he worked with as a member of Hashomer Hatzair in Łódź, Poland. William Cygelfarb was in the Łódź and Piotrków ghettos, as well as in the Buchenwald and Dora-Mittelbau concentration camps, and survived a death march. After the war he returned to Łódź and worked as a Hashomer Hatzair counselor for formerly hidden children. He emigrated to Canada in 1950.
