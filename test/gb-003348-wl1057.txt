Papers re documentary film about the Anschluss

2 boxes

These papers were created during the course of the making of a film about the Anschluss, which was shown on the German television station, ZDF, in 1988.<br /><br />The bulk of the material consists of transcripts of interviews of a variety of people who experienced the Anschluss ranging from modern well-known personalities, former resistance members, former Nazis and diplomats. In addition there are notes on the programme design and biographical notes and cuttings on the interviewees.
