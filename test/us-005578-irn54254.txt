Oral history interview with Reine Tata Chouraqui

3 digital files, WAV

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Peggy Frankston, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the oral history interview with Reine Tata Chouraqui on Feb. 19, 2013 in her home near Marseille, France. This interview is part of a series of USHMM-produced interviews about Jewish life in countries in North Africa during the Holocaust.
