Oral commentary of Russell Bryant and Erich Wolf during their return visit to Europe

14 sound recordings,

Lynne Sher donated the recordings of her uncle Russell Bryant and father Erich Wolf to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in May 2018. The audio files are digitized recordings of 8mm reel-to-reel tapes made by Russell Bryant and Erich Wolf in August 1965 during their return visit to Europe for the first time since WWII.
