Oral history interview with Joseph Schein

2 videocassettes (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Joseph Schein on July 30, 1992, October 22, 1992, November 12, 1992, December 17, 1992, and February 15, 1993. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in October 2000.
