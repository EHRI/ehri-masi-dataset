Hilfsverein der Juden in Deutschland: Correspondence with the Council of German Jewry

6 folders

The provenance of this material is unknown.

The <em>Hilfsverein der Juden in Deutschland</em> was an organisation established by German Jews in 1901 to engage in social welfare and educational activities among needy Jews. It remained in operation until 1941, and during the nazi period it assisted German Jews trying to emigrate.
