Association Cultuelle les Temoins de Jehovah de France

Photocopy of a letter written by Genevieve de Gaulle, dated August 8, 1945, describing the treatment she saw in Ravensbrück concentration camp meted out to Jehovah's Witnesses when they engaged in civil disobedience and refused to work in war-related industries.
