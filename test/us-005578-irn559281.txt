Oral history interview with Nathan Langburt

1 digital file, MPEG-4

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Ina Navazelskis, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the oral history interview with Nathan Langburt on March 2, 2017 in Aventura, FL. Mr. Langburt's younger brother Harry Langburt shares some thoughts at the end of the interview.
