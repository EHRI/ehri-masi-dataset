Le P. Tacchi Venturi au cardinal Maglione

Tacchi Venturi reports to Maglione about his meeting with Mussolini. They discussed about the status of converted-to-Catholicism Italians of Jewish origins and their children. The annexe is the list of proposal presented by Tacchi Venturi to Mussolini. 
Tacchi Venturi suggests:
- to let mixed marriages whose engagement was earlier to the promulgation of the racial laws;
- to let non-State schools hire those who converted before the promulgation of the racial laws;
- to let mixed families have Christian servants.
