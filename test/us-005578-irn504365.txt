Memories of Anna Tempelsman

folder

Describes Anna Lichtmann’s (née Tempelsman, b. 1921) childhood in Zólkiew, Poland (Z︠H︡ovkva, Ukraine); separation from her family and her experiences in Soviet-occupied Poland; her father's and brother's escape to Argentina; a reunion with her mother; hiding from Aktionen; her deportation to Germany to work for a German couple; death of family members; and her liberation and immigration to Argentina in 1946.
