Oral history interview with Israel Unikowski

2 sound cassettes (60 min.),

Library of Congress - Motion Picture, Broadcasting and Recorded Sound Division (MBRS)

David P. Boder conducted the interview with Israel Unikowski on August 2, 1946, at Chateau de Boucicaut near Paris, France. Boder recorded the interview in German, and Bernard Wolf translated the first draft of the interview into English. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum acquired a copy of the interview from the Library of Congress in 1998.
