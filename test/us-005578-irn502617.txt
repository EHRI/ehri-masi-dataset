Oral history interview with Bertha Goldwasser

1 sound cassette (60 min.),

Library of Congress - Motion Picture, Broadcasting and Recorded Sound Division (MBRS)

David P. Boder conducted the interview with Bertha Goldwasser in Paris, France, on August 4, 1946. Boder recorded the interview in German and Bernard Wolf later created an English-language transcript of the interview. Donald Proctor served as editor. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum acquired a copy of the interview from the Library of Congress in 1998.
