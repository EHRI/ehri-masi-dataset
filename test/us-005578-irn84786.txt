Shomer ha-tsaʻir (Organization : Israel). Merkaz tiʻud ṿa-ḥeḳer

Ya'akov Hazan (1899-1992) was an Israeli politician and social activist. He was a central figure Kibutz Haartzi movement and one of the founders of MAPAM party.

Personal archives of Yaakov Hazan (1899-1992) contains his personal documents, correspondence, publications in various newspapers and magazines on Zionism, the HeHalutz and Labor Movement.
