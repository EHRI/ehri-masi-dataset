Oral history interview with Mary Kartal

2 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

American Jewish Archives

The Jewish Holocaust Survivors, Cincinnati Chapter, conducted the interview with Mary Kartal on February 6, 1991. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Joyce Wise, oral history project director for the Jewish Holocaust Survivors, Cincinnati Chapter, on August 2, 1993.
