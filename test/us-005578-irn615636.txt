Minard family liberation photograph collection

folder

1

Collection of 14 photographic prints and postcards documenting the Buchenwald concentration camp immediately following liberation; all captioned on verso “Buchenwald Stalag”; includes images of survivors, victims, skin with tattoos, exterior and interior of buildings, an effigy of Hitler, and American soldiers; dated April 1945. Also includes photographic postcards documenting victims inside and next to the open railcars of the Dachau death train; dated May 1945
