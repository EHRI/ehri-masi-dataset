Le cardinal Maglione au délégué apostolique à Londres Godfrey

Not available.

Maglione updates Godfrey about 11 Catholics of Jewish origins living in the United Kingdom and willing to emigrate to Brazil. Maglione says that these people have to contact their competent consulate. Maglione also informs Godfrey that the Brazilian Embassy stated that no visas will be granted to Jews who were baptised after 1937.
