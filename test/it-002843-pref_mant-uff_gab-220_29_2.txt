Folder n° 220, file n° 29.2 (Sig. Coen Clara, domanda di certificato di perseguitata; Levi Clara in Coen. Dichiarazione di perseguitata razziale)

16 pages

Clara Levi, born in Modena, Italy, on August 27th, 1921, married to Roberto Coen, was a Jewish teacher in Mantua who asked for an official certificate as racial persecuted in 1960. In 1943 or 1944 she went to Switzerland to escape fascist and nazi persecution; in 1944 she gave birth to a baby in Mendrisio, Switzerland.
