Oral history interview with Henia Shylit

1 digital file, MP3

Danielle Shylit donated the oral history interview with her grandmother Henia Shylit to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in January 2021. Danielle Shylit produced the interview in spring 2002 when she was a middle school humanities teacher at Sparhawk School in Amesbury, MA. Students in her Holocaust elective class conducted the interview by telephone.
