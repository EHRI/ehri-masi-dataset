Video footage of the pre-Chabannes reunion with Gerda Bikales, Norbert Bikales, Charles Norman, Ruth Keller, Ernst Rosner, Edith Rosner, Inge Roman, Wolfgang Blumenreich, Miriam Blumenreich, Jerry Gerard, Ossi Goldstein, and Charles Roman

5 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Lisa Gossels and Dean Wetherell, producers and directors of the documentary "The Children of Chabannes" (1999), donated the video footage of the pre-Chabannes reunion with Gerda Bikales, Norbert Bikales, Charles Norman, Ruth Keller, Ernst Rosner, Edith Rosner, Inge Roman, Wolfgang Blumenreich, Miriam Blumenreich, Jerry Gerard, Ossi Goldstein, and Charles Roman used in the making of the film to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in June 2014.
