Oral history interview with Chanoch Davidovich Ginzburg

4 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak, project director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Chanoch Ginzburg on August 7, 1995. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in September 1995.
