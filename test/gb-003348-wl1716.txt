Marion Roth: personal papers

2 folders

Marion Roth (née Rochmann) was born to a Jewish family in 1925 in Berlin. She came to England on a 'Kindertransport' in February 1939, aged 13. Her parents Karl and Else Rochmann (née Imbach) were deported from Berlin to Poland in 1942 where they perished. Marion Roth married her husband in 1946 and became a British citizen. She had two children.

This collection contains the personal papers and correspondence of Marion Roth who was subject to Nazi persecution of Jews and emigrated on a 'Kindertransport' to England in 1939. The material relates to Marion Roth's restitution claim, inheritance and pension.
