Deutsches Postzeitung

The German newspaper was donated to the United States Hocaust Memorial Museum in 1995 by Brigadier General Evelyn P. Foote (Ret.).

Nazi propaganda newspaper published by the postal service overseen by the NSDAP or Nazi Party with the headline: "Versailles! zum 15. Jahrestag des Unterzeichnung des Versailles Diktats" [Versailles! Fifteenth anniversary of the signing of the Treaty of Versailles].
