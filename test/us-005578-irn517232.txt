Ticho family papers

folder

1

The papers consist of a letter delivered by hand to Anna Weinrebova [donor's mother] from her friends, Adela and Vilem Ticho, describing events transpiring during the Holocaust in Czechoslovakia and two death notices printed after World War II for Vilem Ticho and for Rudy and Pepika (Vilem and Adela Ticho's children) who perished in the Holocaust and sent to Anna Weinrebova.
