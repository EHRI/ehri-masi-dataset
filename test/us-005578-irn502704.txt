Oral history interview with Hadar ben Zion

1 videocassette (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak conducted the interview with Hadar ben Zion in Israel on October 27, 1993, for the Israel Documentation Project. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview by transfer from the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Dept. in February 1995.
