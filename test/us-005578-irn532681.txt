"Scouting and Resistance"

Oral testimony of Liliane Klein-Lieber

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Isabelle Pleskoff, head librarian at the Musée d'art et d'histoire du judaïsme (Mahj), donated video recordings of Holocaust presentations and interviews produced by the Mahj to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 2015. The interview was conducted on September 6, 2015 and the original French title is "Scoutisme et résistance avec Liliane Klein-Lieber."
