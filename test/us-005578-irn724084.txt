Renee Curtis donated her oral history interview with her parents Ilona (Gityu) and Ernest Mittelman to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in May 2021. The interview was used as the basis of the book "The Life and Times of Ilona and Ernest Mittelman" by Renee Curtis.
