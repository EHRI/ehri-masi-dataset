Oral history interview with Royce Higa

1 videocassette (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

Hawaii Holocaust Project, University of Hawaii at Manoa

Judy Weightman, of the Hawaii Holocaust Project, conducted the interview with Royce Higa on May 12, 1989 as part of a project to interview Holocaust survivors and concentration camp liberators currently living in Hawaii. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received a copy of the interview in March 1990.
