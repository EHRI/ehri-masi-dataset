Alfred Gruenspecht (Wüstensachsen, Germany, 23 Nov. 1920 - ) was born to a Jewish family. He and members of his family immigrated to the United States in June 1937.

The memoir describes Alfred Gruenspecht's childhood in Wüstensachsen, Germany; his family's experiences in Germany before the Holocaust; his immigration to the United States; his family's assimilation; the death of his parents; and the growth of the Gruenspecht family.
