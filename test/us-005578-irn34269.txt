Schaeffer family papers

folder

1

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Contains a passport issued by the Polish Consulate in Hamburg to Helena Schaeffer, dated December 9, 1936; includes visas to the UK and USA and transit visas to France and Belgium. Also includes a vaccination certificate dated May 23, 1937 for Susi Schaeffer; two marriage certificates issued to Siegfried Schaeffer and Helena Both in Koln on June 8, 1927; a naturalization certificate issued to Sidney Schaeffer on February 25, 1946; and a naturalization certificate issued to Susie Schaeffer on February 25, 1946.
