Correspondence with Berendsohn, Walter A.

18 letters

Prof. Dr. Walter Arthur Berendsohn (1884-1984) was a Jewish-German scholar of Literature Studies. Being dismissed from university in 1933, he emigrated to Denmark and later to Sweden. He is regarded a pioneer in the field of Germen exile literature. See Walk, J., , Munich et. al., K. G. Saur, 1988, p. 27.

Correspondence regarding research assistance for Berendsohn, primarily on the subject of Jewish-German emigrants.
