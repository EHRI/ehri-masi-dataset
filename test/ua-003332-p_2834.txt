Nursing home at the Romny town administration

14 files

Collection contains orders of the German command and the chairman of the city administration on the order of allocation land and gardens to the population, on the taxes. Property lists. Information on the payment of wages. Lists of disabled persons.

Collection includes lists of the Jewish patients of the Romny nursing home (file 3, pp. 2,12), and materials about admission of the Jews to the nursing home (file 3, pp. 13-19).
