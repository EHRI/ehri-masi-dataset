Vilniaus miesto savivaldybė

6 subfonds and 167027 files. Some documents are handwritten.

This collection holds "documents dealing with the use of the Vilnius Ghetto inmates for work, payrolls, food cards for Jews, lists of the Jews in Vilnius 1 and 2 ghettos (September 1941), requests by gentiles to buy confiscated Jews' property" (Galina Žirikova, Lietuvos centrinio valstybės archyvo fondai : holokausto Lietuvoje tyrimo šaltinis (The Collections of the State Archive of Lithuania: a Source of Research on the Holocaust in Lithuania), Vilnius: Valstybinis Vilniaus Gaono Žydų Muziejus, p. 11. 

The fonds consists of personal documents of residents (154108 personal cards), documents of the municipality concerning everyday life, applications from residents about various matters, list of owners of private property, register of the administrative and criminal cases in the district, documentation about prisoners of the Vilnius ghetto, about prisoners of war, personal cards of war refugees, list of women who lived in the camp in Vilnius (wives of Soviet soldiers and officers), lists of staff of the municipality, etc.
