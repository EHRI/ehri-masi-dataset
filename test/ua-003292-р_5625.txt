Inventory 1. 10916 files; 1918–1995.

Inventory 2. 30642 files; 1941–1945.

Collection contains personal criminal investigation files on Soviet citizens accused of political or criminal crimes (Inventory 1), and on Soviet citizens who returned to the USSR from German captivity or from being Ostarbeiter (Inventory 2). Some of these persons may have been of Jewish background.
