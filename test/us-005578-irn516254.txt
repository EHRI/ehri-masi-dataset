Etlya Katzman Fisher photograph collection

folder

1

The collection consists of ten photographs relating to Etlya Katzman Fisher and her family's life during the Holocaust in Bender, Moldova (Tighina, Romania) and Krasnodarskiĭ kraĭ, Russia. The photographs include images of Etlya Katzman Fisher's classmates at girls' schools in Romania and the Soviet Union.
