Oral history interview with Lester Libo

2 digital files, WAV

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Noemi J. Szekely-Popescu, on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Oral History Branch, conducted the oral history interview with Lester Libo on August 3, 2012 in Washington, DC. Johanna Neumann conducted the second half of the interview by telephone on October 25, 2012.
