Herbert Kraus

The Dr. Herbert Kraus papers consist of photographs and photocopies of records documenting Dr. Kraus’s work as the deputy chief of the Medical Corps of the People’s Liberation Partisan and Volunteer Army of Yugoslavia. Photographs depict Dr. Kraus, Josip Broz Tito, and Dr. Gojko Nikolis. Photocopies document Kraus’ task of inspecting hospitals and sanatoriums, his work with the Anti-Fascist Council for the National Liberation of Yugoslavia, and recognition for his participation in the Battle of the Sutjeska.
