Oral history interview with Nicholas Nagy-Talavera

2 videocassettes (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Nicholas Nagy-Talavera on August 12, 1992 and September 24, 1992. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in December 2000.
