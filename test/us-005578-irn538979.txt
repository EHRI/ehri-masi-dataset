Oral history interviews with Irène Paillassou and Renée Paillassou

16 videocasettes (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

Lisa Gossels and Dean Wetherell, producers and directors of the documentary "The Children of Chabannes" (1999), donated the oral history interviews with Irène Paillassou and Renée Paillassou and related footage used in the making of the film to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in June 2014.
