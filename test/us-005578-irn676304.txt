Staatsarchiv Hamburg (Germany)

Dr. Siegfried Urias, a German Jewish lawyer, was born September 2, 1895 in Hamburg. His law office was at Jungfernstieg 24 street. Like all Jewish lawyers, Dr. Urias lost his law credentials in November 1938. He successfully applied for permission to practice as a so-called legal adviser ("Rechtskonsulent"). Dr. Urias was able to emigrate in April 1939 to Chile.

Records of the law office of Dr. Siegfried Urias, a German Jewish lawyer in Hamburg. Contains only client files.
