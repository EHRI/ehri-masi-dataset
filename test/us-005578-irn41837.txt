Lillian and Avrum Feintuch collection

folders

3

Photographs, Ketubah (Jewish marriage contract) and immigration papers illustrating the experiences of Livia Rosenfeld and Avrum Feintuch (donors) during the time period surrounding the Holocaust in Hungary and Germany. Included are images from Avrum's pre-war life in Desz, Hungary as well as Livia's pre-war life in Balmazujvaros, Hungary and post-war lives in Western Europe. Both Livia and Avrum were in slave labor camps during the Holocaust. Avrum was deported to Auschwitz-Birkenau, then to sub-camps of Mauthausen. Livia was deported to the Strasshof and Florisdorf slave labor camps in Austria.
