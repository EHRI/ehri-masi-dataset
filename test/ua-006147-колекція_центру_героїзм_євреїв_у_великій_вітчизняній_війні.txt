Колекція Центру «Героїзм євреїв у Великій Вітчизняній війні»

The collection “Jewish Heroism during the Great Patriotic War” Center at the All-Ukrainian Jewish Council started to be created about 25 years ago. It contains names, biographic data, testimonies and recollections by the Soviet Jews - veterans of the Second World War.

- Card index of the Jewish veterans of the Second World War.

- Card index, biographies and photos of the Jews whom "Hero of the Soviet Union" decoration was awarded;

- Card index, biographies and photos of the Jews whom "Orden of Glory" decoration was awarded;
