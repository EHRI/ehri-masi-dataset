Kolportage der "Zionistischen Protokolle"

1 electronic ressource (1 page)

This file contains a letter by Saly Braunschweig, the president of the Jewish community of Zuerich, addressed to the police department of Zurich. In the letter, Braunschweig informs the police department about the publishing of the Protocols of the elder of Zion by the "Nationaler Front" and refers to the prohibition of the publication of the protocols.
