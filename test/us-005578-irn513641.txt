Joel Kaye papers

folder

1

Consists of three black and white photographs with inscriptions written in ink: vintage portrait image of a family in Szcezekociny, Poland, dated 1933, the couple at the center is Cecile and Aron Krakower (donor's parents); a copy of a vintage image of Cecile and Aron Krakower walking along a path, dated circa 1930, Poland; and a vintage portrait of Sara Jastremsky, donor's maternal grandmother, dated circa pre-1933, Bedzin, Poland.
