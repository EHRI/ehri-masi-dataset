Oral history interview with Sonya Schek

2 videocassettes (SVHS), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project conducted the interview with Sonya Schek on December 2, 1994 and December 20, 1994. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from the Bay Area Holocaust Oral History Project in March 2001.
