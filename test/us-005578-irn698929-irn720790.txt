The Survivors' Suite

ECM LP 1085 (Munich, 1977). Music composed by Keith Jarrett. Performed by Keith Jarrett and the American Quartet, including Keith Jarrett, piano, soprano saxophone, recorder, celeste, osi drums; Dewey Redman: tenor saxophone, percussion; Charlie Haden: double-bass; Paul Motian: drums, percussion. Side A, Track 1: The Survivors' Suite (Beginning) Side B, Track 1: The Survivors' Suite (Conclusion)
