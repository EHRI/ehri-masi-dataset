Jewish anti-defamation work in pre-Hitler Germany

1 folder

Nothing is known about the provenance or whereabouts of the original of this report.

A preface to the report by the Jewish Labor Committee describes the author, Julie Meyers, as having been involved in anti-defamation work in pre-Hitler Germany; that she was one time executive secretary of the Jewish Central Verein in Nuremberg and a member of the State Federation of the Jewish communities of Bavaria. At the time of writing she is described as being on the faculty of the New School of Social Research.
