Gelbhaus, Beck and Neumann families papers

oversize box

1

Contains documents and photographs pertaining to the Gelbhaus, Beck and Neumann families. Includes photocopies from Austrian archives of documents related to the Gelbhaus and Neumann family, and documents and photographs related to Juliette Beck's clothing design and custom couture for Princess Valerie. Also includes correspondence from the Butzel family, and photographs.
