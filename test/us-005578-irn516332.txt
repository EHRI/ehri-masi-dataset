Marianne Scialli papers

folder

1

The papers consist of a German passport ("Reisepass") issued to Liselotte Seligmann [donor's mother], her daughter, Marianne [donor], and her son, Ernst Max [donor's brother] in Düsseldorf, Germany; a vaccination certificate ("Impfschein") issued to Marianne Seligmann [donor] for her passport; and a report card from a private Jewish school in Düsseldorf issued to Marianne Seligmann.
