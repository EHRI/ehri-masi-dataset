Geissler and Arp: British military court case material

c39 frames

Provenance unknown

Despite being regarded as ideal material for a position as lecturer at the <i>Reichspresseschule</i>, and being nominally included on the staff of that organisation in 1937, he never took up the post. It is thought that this may have been because he was fired by Goebbels from his post on <i>Der Angriff</i> for stealing from the petty cash.
