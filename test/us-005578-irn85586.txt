Records relating to the experiences of Danish Jews

2,138 digital images, TIFF and JPEG

Dansk Jødisk Museum

Photo albums, scrapbooks and diaries relating to the experiences of Danish Jews during the war, including escape to Sweden and deportation to Theresienstadt, as well as their return home to Denmark; personal papers of Danish Jews, as well as records of the Danish Jewish Community, Danish and Jewish organizations, including: aid organizations, the Jewish Community in Malmö (Sweden), Danish press, and records of the Danish Nazi Party (1888-2008).
