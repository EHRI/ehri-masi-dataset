Αρχείο Πρωτοδικείου Ξάνθης

13 files.

The pre-war archives of the Court of First Instance in Xanthi were destroyed during the Bulgarian Occupation (1941-1944).

The Court of First Instance in Xanthi was founded in 1920, after the liberation of the city.

The files are relevant to the Bulgarian occupation, war crimes, Greek collaborators, war trials and Bulgarian reparations.
