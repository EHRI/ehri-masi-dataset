Oral history interview with Abraham Szyller

1 DVD,

National Aeronautics and Space Administration

E. Paul Foerman, on behalf of National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) Stennis Space Center, donated a copy of Abraham Szyller's oral testimony to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's Oral History Branch in November 2011. The testimony was produced by the Naval Oceanographic Office (NAVO) in cooperation with NASA Stennis Space Center for NAVO's annual Holocaust Rembembrance Ceremony on May 12, 2011.
