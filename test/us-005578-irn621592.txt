Levy family papers

folder

1

Collection of letters, postcards, and photographs regarding the Levy family of Breisach, Germany. Includes letters written by the donor's grandparents from the Rivesaltes and Gurs camps, addressed to the donor's mother in England, as well as Red Cross letters. Includes photographs of Levy family, who were deported from Breisach and most of whom perished in Auschwitz.
