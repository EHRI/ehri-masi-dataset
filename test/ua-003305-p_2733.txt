Nerubaivka village administration in Pidvysotsk district

10 files

Collection contains Orders by Kirovohrad Gebietskommissariat, Podvysotsk district state administration, and district councils on administrative and economic issues, mobilization of the labor force and its export to Germany, the fight against the partisan movement, the mandatory supply of agricultural products, the labor duty and taxation of the population. Information on the population of the village. Lists of persons who arrived to the village. Lists of persons who were in service in the German army.

Collection includes orders to register the Jewish population and to impose 20% tax on them (file 5).
