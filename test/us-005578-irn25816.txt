Slide depicting New York City Mayor, La Guardia, with a female dinner guest (who the caption implies to be a Jew)

Height: 1.930 inches (4.902 cm) | Width: 1.930 inches (4.902 cm) | Depth: 4.880 inches (12.395 cm)

The slide was donated to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 1995 by Mortimer Schaffer.

Obtained by an American G.I. in a home during the final stages of the war in Europe.
