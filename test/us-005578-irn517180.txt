Henryk Zvi and Jutta Bergman papers

folder

1

The papers consist of five photographs of images of Jutta Szmirgeld Bergman, her brother, Simon, and their mother; four photographs of images of the Bergman family before World War II in Łódź, Poland; an invitation for the marriage of Jutta Szmirgeld and Henryk Zvi Bergman on July 2, 1946; and a document issued to Jutta Bergman that permits her to reside in Palestine as a legal immigrant.
