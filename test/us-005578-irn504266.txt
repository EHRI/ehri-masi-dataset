During World War II, Gerald C. ("Babe") Caskey was a First Lieutenant; he served in the U.S. Army with the XII Corps, 4th Armored Division, Third Army, and he was lent to the 27th AAA, 771st Armored Battalion, Seventh Army.

Consists of a copy of a letter (dated 10 May 1945) from Gerald C. ("Babe") Caskey to Norma ("Normie") Caskey relating Gerald's feelings about seeing Dachau.
