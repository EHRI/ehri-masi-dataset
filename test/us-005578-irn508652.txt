Oral history interview with Lory Grunberger Cahn

4 sound cassettes (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Lory Grunberger Cahn in Philadelphia, Pa., on May 4, 1981. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on September 22, 1998.
