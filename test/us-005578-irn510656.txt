Oral history interview with Fred Baron

2 sound cassettes (90 min.),

Jewish Community Relations Council, Anti-Defamation League of Minnesota and the Dakotas

The interview with Fred Baron was conducted by Rhoda G. Lewin on January 13, 1984 as part of a Holocaust oral history project sponsored by the Jewish Community Relations Council, Anti-Defamation League of Minnesota and the Dakotas. The interview was acquired by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 1992.
