Oral history interview with Karessa (Kay) Foldvary

1 sound cassette (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Karessa (Kay) Foldvary in Philadelphia, Pa., on July 6, 1989. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on September 22, 1998.
