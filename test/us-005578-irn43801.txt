Paul H. Taylor papers

folder

1

Consists of documents related to visiting Israeli dignitaries in New York City in the 1950s. Includes minutes of a meeting in preparation for a reception hosted by the city in honor of David Ben-Gurion in 1951, a letter preparing for the arrival of Shlomo Z. Shragai in March 1952, and a photograph with caption and press release of Shalom Kahalny, a marathon runner, carrying a torch of freedom in 1956.
