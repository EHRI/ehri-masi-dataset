Oral history interview with Hilda Thieberger

6 sound cassettes (60 min.),

Jewish Community Council of Greater Washington

The interview with Hilda Thieberger was conducted on July 25, 1989 as part of the Jewish Community Council of Greater Washington's oral history project to document Washington, DC area survivor's experiences of the Holocaust. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the interview on May 26, 1993.
