Man who shot Swiss Nazi Chief. A new picture just received in London of David Frankfurter, the young Jewish medical student, who shot dead Wilhelm Gustloff, leader of the German Nazis in Switzerland, at Gustloff's home in Davos, Switzerland. Frakfurter, who is said to be suffering from an incurable bone disease, afterwards surrendered to the police.
