[Two letters written by Du Chayla to Georges Brunschvig]

1 electronic ressource (3 pages)

This file contains two letters written in French by Du Chayla to Georges Brunschvig. Alexandre du Chayla was a French nobleman who converted to Russian Orthodoxy. He gave crucial evidence and testimonies for the prosecution at the Bern trials. The first one is a very short letter written on the 20th of October 1937. The second one is a 2 page long letter that is also addressed to Georges Brunschvig. Both contain information by Du Chayla about the Protocols.
