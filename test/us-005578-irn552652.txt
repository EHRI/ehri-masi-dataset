William B. Schneider (1922-2009) was born January 31, 1922, to Joseph and Francis Schneider. He had three brothers, Jerry, Robert, and John. He served as a medic in the Army during WWII in Europe. He was in Normandy during the invasion and served in France and Germany. Bill married Betty Zywicki in 1946 and the couple raised their children in Winona, MN. They had four daughters, Carol, Maribeth, Chris, and Sherry, and two sons, Paul and Mark.

Immediate postwar photographs of the removal of corpses and reburial in mass grave at Dora-Mittelbau.
