Oral history interview with Flora Singer

2 videocasettes (Betacam SP) (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

6 videocasettes (Betacam SP) (Betacam SP), sound, color ; 1/2 in.

The Holocaust Eyewitness Project produced the interview with Flora Singer on August 4, 1988 in the Washington, D.C. area. Edith Fierst, on behalf of the Holocaust Eyewitness Project, donated a copy of the interview to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 1989.
