Oral history interview with Jola (Yetta) Gross

1 sound cassette (60 min.),

Library of Congress - Motion Picture, Broadcasting and Recorded Sound Division (MBRS)

David P. Boder conducted the interview with Jola (Yetta) Gross on August 2, 1946, in France. Boder recorded the interview in German, and Bernard Wolf assisted with the first draft translation of the interview into English. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum acquired a copy of the interview from the Library of Congress in 1998.
