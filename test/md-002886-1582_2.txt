Lǎpuşna commission for checking the files of the public sector employees that remained on the territory of Bessarabia in 1940-1941

199 items

Declassified files

The commissions were established to verify the loyalty to the Romanian state of former employees who held public posts who remained in the territory of Bessarabia in the years 1940-1941 (during the Soviet rule)

Personal files of officials, church singers, priests, officials of the financial administration, clergymen, railway workers, etc. who remained in the territory of Bessarabia in 1940-1941 (during the Soviet rule)
