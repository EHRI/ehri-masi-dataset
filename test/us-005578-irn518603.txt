Consists of one article, entitled "Zu Besuch in der Alten Heimat: Eine Rückschau auf meinen letzten Besuch im Burgenland (Stadtschlaining, Jabing, Oberwart)" and English translation of the same article, entitled "A Personal Account of my Last Visit to Burgenland (Stadtschlaining, Jabing, Oberwart)", by Regina Löwy Espenshade. The article contains reflections on her family's pre-war and wartime experiences, her genealogical research, and her favorable impressions of efforts in Stadtschlaining to commemorate both the Jewish community and the Holocaust.
