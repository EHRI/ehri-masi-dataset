Nazi Force Aged Jews to Scrub Sidewalks in Vienna - Nazi troopers and members of the Nazi Party amusedly watch as olderly Jews are forced to scrub the sidewalks of the main streets of Vienna. This was but one of the many cruelties inflicted on non-Nazis and was intended to redicule those of Jewish faith of parentage who later were forced to live in ghettos and many of whom were thrown into concentration camps and killed in special extermination camps.
