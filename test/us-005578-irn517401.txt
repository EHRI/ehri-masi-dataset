Fest der Jugend [Festival of Youth] pin

overall: Height: 2.000 inches (5.08 cm) | Width: 1.000 inches (2.54 cm) | Depth: 0.125 inches (0.318 cm)

The pin was donated to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in 2005 by the University of Mary Washington Galleries.

German pin commemorating a youth festival held by the Hitler Youth in 1933.
