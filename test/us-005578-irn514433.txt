Oral history interview with Chancia Szczenshliwy Tuchmayer

1 sound cassette (60 min.),

Harry Tuchmayer donated the oral history of his mother, Chancia Szczenshliwy Tuchmayer, to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum on January 9, 2004. The interview was conducted by Mrs. Tuchmayer's daughter-in-law, Kathleen Berkeley, on April 18, 1984.
