Commemorative material re nazi victims of Kiel

1 folder

The plan to create two memorial tablets in the memorial hall of the Kieler Gelehrtenschule to commemorate these individuals was the idea of Hartmut Kunkel, friend of the depositor, and history teacher at the German School in Richmond, London, 1984-1988.

This collection of copy material relates to the commemoration of former Jewish residents of Kiel, Friedrich Schumm and Hillert Luecken,who died at the hands of the Nazis.
