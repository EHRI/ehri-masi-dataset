The interviewee talks about his childhood in Amsterdam South where he grew up amidst the Jewish population. He talks about his Jewish friends, about a roundup in his street and how he secretly entered an abandoned house. He also describes a few situations he experienced together with his father; he describes Germans at Dam Square in Amsterdam, Dolle Dinsdag (September 4, 1944), and the Jewish neighbourhood where all the wood was stolen.
