"A French Tunisian Communist Jew"

Oral testimony of Elie-Lucien Uzan

1 digital file, MPEG-4

Isabelle Pleskoff, head librarian at the Musée d'art et d'histoire du judaïsme (Mahj), donated video recordings of Holocaust presentations and interviews produced by the Mahj to the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum in October 2015. The interview was conducted on December 8, 2013 and the original French title is "Un juif tunisien français communiste avec Elie-Lucien Uzan."
