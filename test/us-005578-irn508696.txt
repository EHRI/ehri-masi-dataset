Oral history interview with Elsa Turteltaub

1 sound cassette (60 min.),

Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive

The Gratz College Holocaust Oral History Archive conducted the interview with Elsa Turteltaub in New York, NY on July 14, 1987. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum received the tapes of the interview from Gratz College on September 30, 1999.
