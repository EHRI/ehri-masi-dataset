Erich Weinert: Rote Lieder Rote Gedichte

Erich Weinert (1890-1953) was a German Communist writer and a member of the Communist Party of Germany (KPD). Two LPs are in a 29-page book cover with two jackets for the discs. Title on front: 'Rote Lieder Gedichte', title on labels and inside book: 'Rote Lieder - Rote Gedichte'. Artwork [Cover] – Werner Knipsel Editor [Redaktion] – Hugo Fetting Lyrics By – Erich Weinert Vocals – Ernst Busch Record 1 Side A: Die grobe Zeit 1914-1918; Sozialdemokratisches Mailiedchen Side B: Die Kaserne winkt; An die Armeen Europas; Der heimliche Aufmarsch Record 2 Side A: Abschied von Spanien; Lied von der Roten Fahne Side B: Die Hakenrune; Roter Wedding
