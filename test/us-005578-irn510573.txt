Oral history interview with Tatyana Samuilovna Gildiner

4 videocassettes (U-Matic), sound, color ; 3/4 in.

United States Holocaust Memorial Museum

Nathan Beyrak, project director for the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, Oral History Branch, coordinated the interview with Tatyana Samuilovna Gildiner on July 31, 1995. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum Archives received the tapes of the interview in September 1995.
