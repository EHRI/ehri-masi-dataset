Press cutting about 'die Jüdische Front'

1 folder

This press cutting from the Schaerdinger Wochenblatt, 12<sup>th</sup> of June 1937, reports a call of the Austrian “Bund Jüdischer Frontsoldaten” in its official periodical “Die Jüdische Front” to defend Jews against antisemitism and to donate money for this aim. It uses this call to connect it with antisemitic stereotypes.
