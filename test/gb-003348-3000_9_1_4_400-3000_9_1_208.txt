Correspondence with British Foreign Office

108 letters

Correspondence mostly about terms of access to archival material (mainly war crime trials related material). Further issues are research and work related information requests, and administration details regarding trips to Germany as well as the invitation of Germans to the UK. Contained are numerous memos on meetings with FO officials, German language leaflets on conferences on British politics, an invitation card to a FO reception of German chancellor Konrad Adenauer, a report about certain records held by the FO, and a draft for a Wiener Library Bulletin piece.
