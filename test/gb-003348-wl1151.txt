Plight of Hungarian jews: Mostly copy documents

1 folder

It is thought that the originals are deposited at the US national archives. The original document reference is repeated (if known) in the descriptions below. The depositor was co-chairman of Brookline, the Holocaust Memorial Committee, based in Massachusetts, USA, and former inmate of Drancy concentration camp.

This collection contains mostly copy documents from the US legation in Stockholm to the US Department of State and concerns the possibility of saving Hungarian Jews during the Nazi era.
